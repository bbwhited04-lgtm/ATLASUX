# Subscription Plans

## Overview

Atlas UX offers four subscription tiers designed to scale with your business. Each tier provides access to the AI agent workforce, with increasing capabilities, agent counts, and support levels as you move up.

## Plan Comparison

### Starter -- $99/month

The Starter plan is designed for individuals and small teams getting started with AI automation.

**Included**:
- Core AI agent access (Atlas, Binky, Cheryl)
- 500 jobs per month
- Basic CRM with contact management
- Knowledge Base (limited storage)
- Email automation (basic templates)
- Community support

**Limitations**:
- 3 active agents
- 50 daily action cap
- $100 auto-spend limit
- 5 budget categories
- No custom integrations
- No priority support

**Best For**: Solopreneurs, freelancers, and micro-businesses testing AI automation.

### Professional -- $249/month

The Professional plan is the most popular choice, designed for growing businesses that need a full AI workforce.

**Included**:
- Full agent roster access
- 2,000 jobs per month
- Advanced CRM with segmentation
- Full Knowledge Base
- Email automation with custom templates
- Social media automation (all 13 publishers)
- Blog publishing pipeline
- Priority email support

**Per-Seat Pricing**: $49.80/seat/month (minimum seats apply)

**Capabilities**:
- 15+ active agents
- 200 daily action cap
- $500 auto-spend limit
- 15 budget categories
- Standard integrations (Microsoft 365, Telegram, Stripe)
- Business Manager with full analytics

**Best For**: Small businesses, marketing agencies, and e-commerce operators who need comprehensive automation.

### Business -- $45/seat/month

The Business plan is designed for teams and organizations that need collaborative AI operations.

**Included**:
- Everything in Professional
- 5,000 jobs per month
- Advanced analytics and reporting
- Custom workflow creation
- Multi-user access with role-based permissions
- Custom integrations (3 per year)
- Phone and email support
- Dedicated account manager

**Capabilities**:
- 25+ active agents
- 500 daily action cap
- $2,000 auto-spend limit
- 25 budget categories
- Advanced security and compliance tools
- Custom workflow templates
- API access for custom development

**Best For**: Mid-size businesses, agencies with multiple clients, and teams requiring collaboration features.

### Enterprise -- $40/seat/month

The Enterprise plan provides maximum capability with custom configuration.

**Included**:
- Everything in Business
- Unlimited jobs
- Custom agent configurations
- White-label options
- Custom AI model routing
- Unlimited custom integrations
- 24/7 priority support
- Dedicated success team
- Custom SLA

**Capabilities**:
- Unlimited active agents
- Custom daily action cap
- Custom auto-spend limit
- Unlimited budget categories
- Advanced security auditing
- Custom governance policies
- On-premise deployment options

**Best For**: Large organizations, enterprise teams, and businesses with specialized compliance or security requirements.

## Billing

### Monthly Billing
All plans are billed monthly by default. Charges are processed through Stripe on the same date each month.

### Annual Billing
Annual billing is available at a discounted rate. Contact sales for annual pricing on Business and Enterprise plans.

### Per-Seat Pricing
Professional, Business, and Enterprise plans use per-seat pricing:
- Professional: $49.80/seat/month
- Business: $45/seat/month
- Enterprise: $40/seat/month
- Minimum seat requirements apply per plan

### Adding Seats
Additional seats can be added at any time:
1. Go to Settings > Subscription
2. Click "Add Seats"
3. Select the number of additional seats
4. Billing adjusts pro-rata for the current period

## Upgrading and Downgrading

### Upgrading
1. Navigate to Settings > Subscription
2. Select the desired plan
3. Complete payment through Stripe
4. New capabilities are available immediately
5. Billing adjusts pro-rata

### Downgrading
1. Navigate to Settings > Subscription
2. Select a lower tier
3. Downgrade takes effect at the end of the current billing period
4. Data is retained but features are limited to the new tier
5. Active jobs above the new tier's limit will complete but new ones will be capped

## FAQ

**Can I try Atlas UX before subscribing?**
The Starter plan provides a low-cost entry point. The free prompt pack on the Store gives you a taste of the platform's capabilities.

**What happens if I exceed my job limit?**
Jobs beyond your monthly limit will be queued but not processed until the next billing cycle or until you upgrade.

**Can I switch plans mid-month?**
Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately with pro-rata billing. Downgrades take effect at the end of the billing period.

**Is there a free trial?**
Contact sales for trial access options. Enterprise plan trials are available for qualified organizations.

**How do per-seat prices work?**
Each seat represents one user with full access to the platform. The base subscription includes a minimum number of seats; additional seats are billed at the per-seat rate.
