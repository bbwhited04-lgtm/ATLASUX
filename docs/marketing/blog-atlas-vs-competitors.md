## The Crowded AI Landscape

If you are evaluating AI tools for your business, you have options. Jasper, Copy.ai, HubSpot AI, ChatGPT, and dozens of others all claim to boost productivity. The question is not "which one generates better text?" The question is "which one actually runs my business operations?"

That distinction matters.

## The Comparison Framework

We are comparing across six dimensions that matter for business operations:

1. **Architecture**: Single AI vs. multi-agent team
2. **Scope**: Content generation vs. full business operations
3. **Governance**: Can you trust it with real business decisions?
4. **Integration**: Does it connect to your existing tools?
5. **Audit Trail**: Do you know what it did and why?
6. **Pricing**: What does it actually cost?
