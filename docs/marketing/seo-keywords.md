## Blog Content Strategy

### Content Pillars

1. **AI Automation Education** (40% of content)
   - How AI automation works
   - Getting started guides
   - Prompt engineering tips
   - Tool comparisons

2. **Business Use Cases** (25% of content)
   - Industry-specific use cases
   - Customer success stories (when available)
   - ROI analyses

3. **Agent Spotlights and Product Updates** (20% of content)
   - Individual agent deep dives
   - New feature announcements
   - Platform updates and changelog

4. **Industry Insights** (15% of content)
   - AI industry trends
   - Small business trends
   - Future of work commentary

### Publishing Cadence
- **Target**: 2 blog posts per week (8 per month)
- **Minimum**: 1 blog post per week (4 per month)
- **Optimal length**: 1,200-2,000 words
- **Every post**: Featured image, meta description, internal links, CTA

### Internal Linking Strategy
Every blog post should include:
- 1-2 links to other blog posts (related topics)
- 1 link to a relevant KB article or feature page
- 1 link to the Store (prompt packs or ebooks, where relevant)
- 1 CTA to sign up or start a trial

### Content Calendar Template

| Week | Post 1 (Educational) | Post 2 (Use Case/Spotlight) |
|------|---------------------|---------------------------|
| 1 | How to automate [function] with AI | Use case: [industry] |
| 2 | [Number] tips for [topic] | Agent spotlight: [agent name] |
| 3 | AI vs traditional: [comparison] | Customer success story |
| 4 | Getting started with [feature] | Product update roundup |

## Technical SEO

### Site Structure
```
/ (Homepage)
/about
/blog
  /blog/category/:category
  /blog/:slug
/store
/product
/privacy
/terms
/acceptable-use
/payment
```

### Page Speed
- Code splitting: react-vendor, router, ui-vendor, charts chunks
- Lazy loading: all app pages and public pages load on demand
- Chunk failure recovery: auto-reload on stale chunk references

### Structured Data Opportunities
- **Organization**: Business name, logo, contact info
- **Blog Posts**: Article schema with author, date, description
- **Products**: Product schema for Store items with pricing
- **FAQ**: FAQ schema for the troubleshooting page
- **Software Application**: SoftwareApplication schema for the platform

### Sitemap and Indexing
- Blog posts should be the primary indexed content
- App routes (behind gate code) should be excluded from indexing
- Store and product pages should be indexed
- Public pages (About, Privacy, Terms) should be indexed
