  MARKETING STRATEGY & PLANNING (10 Prompts)
============================================================

1. COMPLETE MARKETING PLAN GENERATOR

Create a comprehensive 90-day marketing plan for my
[INDUSTRY] business called [BUSINESS NAME] located in
[CITY/REGION]. My target customers are [DESCRIBE IDEAL
CUSTOMER — age, income, interests, pain points]. My monthly
marketing budget is approximately [BUDGET]. Include specific
weekly action items broken into three phases: awareness
(month 1), engagement (month 2), and conversion (month 3),
with recommended channels, content types, and measurable
KPIs for each phase.


2. COMPETITOR ANALYSIS FRAMEWORK

Analyze the competitive landscape for a [INDUSTRY] small
business. My top three competitors are [COMPETITOR 1],
[COMPETITOR 2], and [COMPETITOR 3]. For each competitor,
break down their apparent brand positioning, pricing
strategy, social media presence, website strengths and
weaknesses, and unique selling points. Then identify three
to five gaps or underserved opportunities in the market
that my business [BUSINESS NAME] could exploit to
differentiate itself.


3. BRAND POSITIONING STATEMENT

Help me craft a clear brand positioning statement for
[BUSINESS NAME], a [INDUSTRY] business that serves
[TARGET AUDIENCE]. Our main products/services are
[LIST PRODUCTS OR SERVICES]. What makes us different from
competitors is [KEY DIFFERENTIATOR]. Write the positioning
statement in the format: "For [target customer] who
[need/want], [brand] is the [category] that [key benefit]
because [reason to believe]." Then provide three variations
I can test with customers.


4. IDEAL CUSTOMER AVATAR BUILDER

Build a detailed customer avatar for my [INDUSTRY]
business. My best existing customers tend to be [BRIEF
DESCRIPTION]. Create a full profile including: demographic
details (age, gender, income, location, education),
psychographic details (values, interests, lifestyle,
media consumption habits), their top five pain points
related to [YOUR PRODUCT/SERVICE CATEGORY], what triggers
them to search for a solution, and the objections they
typically have before purchasing.


5. UNIQUE SELLING PROPOSITION DEVELOPMENT

I run a [INDUSTRY] business called [BUSINESS NAME] in
[LOCATION]. My products/services include [LIST OFFERINGS].
My competitors mostly compete on [PRICE / QUALITY /
CONVENIENCE / OTHER]. Help me develop three distinct unique
selling propositions (USPs) that I could test in my
marketing. For each USP, provide: a one-sentence hook, a
supporting paragraph explaining why it matters to the
customer, and a suggestion for how to communicate it across
my website headline, social media bio, and elevator pitch.


6. QUARTERLY GOAL SETTING FOR MARKETING

I own a [INDUSTRY] small business currently generating
approximately [MONTHLY REVENUE] in monthly revenue. My
biggest marketing challenge right now is [DESCRIBE
CHALLENGE — e.g., low website traffic, poor social
engagement, not enough leads]. Help me set three SMART
marketing goals for the next quarter, along with the
specific tactics I should use to achieve each goal, the
tools or platforms I should leverage, and a simple weekly
tracking method I can use to measure progress without
spending more than 15 minutes per week on reporting.


7. MARKETING BUDGET ALLOCATION ADVISOR

I have a monthly marketing budget of [BUDGET] for my
[INDUSTRY] business. My current marketing channels are
[LIST CURRENT CHANNELS — e.g., Facebook ads, Google Ads,
email, local events]. My primary goal this quarter is
[GOAL — e.g., increase foot traffic, grow online sales,
build email list]. Recommend how I should allocate my budget
across channels, including any new channels I should test.
Provide percentage breakdowns, expected cost-per-acquisition
estimates where possible, and a rationale for each
allocation decision.


8. SEASONAL MARKETING CAMPAIGN PLANNER

Plan a seasonal marketing campaign for my [INDUSTRY]
business around [HOLIDAY / SEASON / EVENT — e.g.,
Valentine's Day, Back to School, Black Friday, Summer].
My products/services are [LIST OFFERINGS] and my target
audience is [TARGET AUDIENCE]. Create a timeline starting
[X WEEKS] before the event with specific content to post,
email sequences to send, promotional offers to run, and
in-store or online event ideas. Include estimated timelines
for creative production so nothing feels last-minute.


9. REFERRAL PROGRAM DESIGNER

Design a customer referral program for my [INDUSTRY]
business called [BUSINESS NAME]. My average customer
lifetime value is approximately [CLV OR AVERAGE ORDER
VALUE]. My current customer base is roughly [NUMBER]
people. Create a referral program structure including:
what incentive to offer the referrer, what incentive to
offer the new customer, how to communicate the program
(email, in-store signage, social media), tracking method
recommendations, and three example referral messages
customers could share with friends.


10. ANNUAL MARKETING CALENDAR TEMPLATE

Create a 12-month marketing calendar framework for my
[INDUSTRY] business targeting [TARGET AUDIENCE] in
[LOCATION/MARKET]. Include monthly themes that align with
seasonal trends relevant to my industry, key promotional
periods, content pillars I should focus on each month,
recommended email sends per month, and social media posting
frequency. Format it as a month-by-month outline I can
paste into a spreadsheet, with columns for: Month, Theme,
Key Dates, Content Focus, Promotions, and Email Campaigns.

19. SOCIAL MEDIA CRISIS AND NEGATIVE COMMENT RESPONSES

YOUR INDUSTRY], a comment accusing my pricing of being too
high, a customer publicly posting about a bad experience,
a competitor or troll leaving inflammatory comments, and a
misunderstanding about my product/service. Each response
should be 2-4 sentences, acknowledge the person's concern,
offer to resolve it privately, and protect my brand

20. HOLIDAY AND TRENDING CONTENT IDEAS
