# How Lucy Works

Lucy is your AI receptionist. She answers every phone call to your business, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week -- including nights, weekends, and holidays. No more missed calls. No more voicemail. No more losing customers because nobody picked up.

## What Lucy actually does

When someone calls your business number, Lucy picks up on the first ring. She greets the caller by your business name, listens to what they need, and takes action. Depending on the situation, she will:

- **Book an appointment** directly on the call and confirm the details
- **Send an SMS confirmation** to the caller so they have the appointment info in writing
- **Take a detailed message** if the caller needs to reach a specific person
- **Answer common questions** about your services, hours, and pricing
- **Notify you on Slack** so you see every call and message in real time

Lucy handles all of this while the caller is still on the phone. There is no "we'll get back to you" -- she resolves the call right then and there.

## How does she sound?

Lucy uses ElevenLabs voice technology, which means she sounds like a real person having a real conversation. She is not the robotic voice you hear on automated phone trees. She speaks naturally, uses contractions, matches the caller's energy, and adjusts her tone depending on the situation. A frustrated caller gets patience and empathy. A quick caller gets straight answers.

You can learn more about voice options in the [Voice Profile](voice-profile.md) guide.

## How does she know about my business?

During setup, you tell Lucy about your business -- your name, services, hours of operation, and any other details you want her to know. She uses this information in every call. When a plumber's customer calls asking about drain cleaning, Lucy knows that is a service you offer. When a salon client calls at 7pm on a Tuesday, Lucy knows whether you are open or closed and responds accordingly.

You can customize her personality and vocabulary too. See [Personality Customization](personality-customization.md) for details.

## What happens after a call?

After every call, Lucy logs the details: who called, what they needed, and what action she took. If she booked an appointment, you will see it in your calendar. If she took a message, it shows up in your Slack channel and your Atlas UX dashboard. Nothing falls through the cracks.

## Is she always available?

Yes. Lucy does not take breaks, call in sick, or go on vacation. She answers calls at 2am on a Sunday the same way she answers at 10am on a Monday. Even outside your business hours, she picks up, lets the caller know your hours, and offers to take a message or book them for the next available time.

See the [Business Hours](business-hours.md) guide to set up how Lucy behaves during and after hours.

## Who is Lucy for?

Lucy is built for trade and service businesses -- plumbers, HVAC techs, electricians, salons, barber shops, cleaning companies, and similar businesses where missing a call means missing revenue. If your phone rings while you are on a job site, under a sink, or cutting someone's hair, Lucy handles it.

## Getting started

Setting up Lucy takes about 10 minutes. The setup wizard walks you through everything: your business info, services, hours, and voice preferences. See [Setup Wizard](../getting-started/setup-wizard.md) to get started.
