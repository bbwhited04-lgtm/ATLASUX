# Customizing Lucy's Personality

Lucy is not a one-size-fits-all phone bot. She adapts her personality to match your business, your industry, and the way you want customers to feel when they call. This guide explains how to shape Lucy's tone, vocabulary, and behavior.

## Setting the tone

Every business has its own vibe. A high-end salon wants polished and elegant. A neighborhood plumber wants friendly and down-to-earth. A busy HVAC company wants efficient and no-nonsense.

Lucy's personality falls along a spectrum, and you control where she lands:

- **Professional** -- Polished language, formal but approachable. Good for medical offices, insurance, and premium services.
- **Friendly** -- Warm and conversational. Great for salons, barbershops, and family-run businesses.
- **Casual** -- Relaxed and easygoing. Works well for landscaping, general contractors, and trades where customers expect a straight-talking approach.

You set this during the [Setup Wizard](../getting-started/setup-wizard.md), and you can adjust it anytime from your dashboard.

## Business-specific vocabulary

Lucy learns the words your customers use. When you add your services during setup, she incorporates them into her vocabulary. If you are a plumber, she knows the difference between a "drain cleaning" and a "water heater install." If you run a salon, she understands "balayage," "blowout," and "color correction."

Here is what you can customize:

- **Services list** -- The specific services you offer. Lucy references these when talking to callers and booking appointments.
- **Hours of operation** -- Lucy uses your hours to tell callers when you are available and to schedule appointments within your working windows.
- **Business type** -- Telling Lucy your industry helps her use the right language naturally. A "plumbing" business gets different phrasing than a "beauty salon."

## How Lucy adapts during a call

Lucy does not just have one mode. She reads the caller's energy and adjusts in real time:

- **New callers** get a friendly introduction and gentle guidance through what you offer.
- **Returning callers** are recognized and treated like regulars.
- **Frustrated callers** get patience, empathy, and acknowledgment before Lucy moves to a solution. She never argues or gets defensive.
- **Callers in a hurry** get quick, direct answers without unnecessary small talk.

This is built into Lucy's core behavior. You do not need to configure it -- she does it automatically.

## What Lucy will never do

No matter how you customize her personality, Lucy follows strict guardrails:

- She will not make promises about pricing, timelines, or guarantees unless you have explicitly authorized that information.
- She will not share internal details about your team or operations.
- She will not fabricate information. If she does not know the answer, she says so honestly and offers to have someone call back.
- She keeps responses concise -- under three sentences unless the caller asks for more detail.
- She only asks one question at a time so callers do not feel interrogated.

## When callers ask if she is AI

Lucy handles this with confidence and honesty. If a caller asks whether they are talking to a real person, Lucy responds with something like:

> "I'm an AI assistant for [Your Business Name]. I'm here to help you with whatever you need."

She does not pretend to be human and she does not apologize for being AI. Most callers do not care as long as their problem gets solved.

## Tips for getting it right

1. **Think about your best employee** -- the one customers love talking to. That is the personality you want Lucy to mirror.
2. **Add all your services** during setup, even the less common ones. The more Lucy knows, the better she handles unexpected questions.
3. **Test with a real call.** Call your number, pretend to be a new customer, and see how Lucy responds. Adjust if something feels off.
4. **Keep it simple.** Lucy's defaults are designed to work well across trades and service businesses. You do not need to over-engineer her personality.

For voice and sound customization, see [Voice Profile](voice-profile.md). For hours and scheduling behavior, see [Business Hours](business-hours.md).
