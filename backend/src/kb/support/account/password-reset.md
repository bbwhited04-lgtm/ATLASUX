# Password Reset

Forgot your password? It happens to everyone. Resetting it takes about two minutes, and you do not need to contact support. Here is how.

## How to Reset Your Password

### Step 1: Go to the Login Page

Open Atlas UX in your browser and click on the login page. Below the email and password fields, you will see a **Forgot Password?** link. Click it.

### Step 2: Enter Your Email Address

Type in the email address you use to log in to Atlas UX. This is the same email you used when you created your account. Click **Send Reset Link**.

### Step 3: Check Your Email

Within a few minutes, you will receive an email from Atlas UX with a password reset link. Open the email and click the link. It will take you to a page where you can set a new password.

**Tip:** The reset link expires after a limited time for security reasons. If it expires before you use it, just go back to the login page and request a new one.

### Step 4: Set a New Password

Enter your new password and confirm it. Once you click **Save** or **Reset Password**, you are done. Log in with your new password.

## Tips for a Strong Password

Your password protects your business data, call history, customer information, and billing details. Make it a good one:

- **At least 8 characters** -- Longer is better. A 12-character password is significantly harder to crack than an 8-character one.
- **Mix it up** -- Use a combination of uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols.
- **Avoid the obvious** -- Do not use your business name, "password123", your phone number, or your pet's name. Attackers try these first.
- **Use a password manager** -- Tools like 1Password, Bitwarden, or the one built into your browser can generate and remember strong passwords for you. You only need to remember one master password.

## Did Not Receive the Reset Email?

If the email does not show up within a few minutes:

1. **Check spam and junk folders** -- Email filters sometimes catch automated messages. Look for an email from Atlas UX or our email domain.
2. **Verify the email address** -- Make sure you typed the exact email you used to sign up. A typo means the reset goes to the wrong address (or nowhere).
3. **Wait a moment** -- Email delivery can take a few minutes depending on your provider. Give it up to 10 minutes before trying again.
4. **Try again** -- Go back to the Forgot Password page and request another link. The old one will be invalidated.
5. **Contact support** -- If you have tried the above and still cannot get a reset email, reach out to our support team. We can verify your account and help you get back in.

## Changing Your Password (When You Know Your Current One)

If you know your current password but want to change it -- maybe for security reasons or because you started using a password manager -- you can do this from your account settings:

1. Log in to your Atlas UX dashboard
2. Go to **Settings** and then **Account**
3. Click **Change Password**
4. Enter your current password, then your new password
5. Click **Save**

## Keeping Your Account Secure

A strong password is your first line of defense, but it is not the only one. Consider these additional steps:

- **Enable two-factor authentication** -- Adds a second layer of security beyond your password. See [Security Settings](security-settings.md) for setup instructions.
- **Do not share your login** -- If team members need access, add them as separate users with their own credentials. See [Team Members](team-members.md).
- **Log out on shared devices** -- If you log in from a computer that others use, always log out when you are done.

## Still Locked Out?

If you cannot access your email and cannot reset your password, contact our support team. We will verify your identity and help you regain access to your account. We may ask for information like your business name, the email on file, or your last billing date to confirm you are the account owner.
