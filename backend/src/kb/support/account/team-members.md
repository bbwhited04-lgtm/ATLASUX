# Team Members

You do not have to manage Atlas UX alone. Add your office manager, business partner, or key employees so everyone stays in the loop on calls, appointments, and messages. Each person gets their own login -- no sharing passwords.

## Adding a Team Member

1. Log in to your Atlas UX dashboard
2. Go to **Settings** and then **Team**
3. Click **Add Member**
4. Enter their name and email address
5. Select a role (Admin, Manager, or Viewer)
6. Click **Send Invite**

The new team member receives an email invitation with a link to set up their password and log in. The invite expires after a set period, so let them know to check their email soon.

## Roles and Permissions

Every team member gets a role that controls what they can see and do. Here is what each role includes:

### Admin

Full access to everything. Admins can:

- View all calls, messages, and appointments
- Manage Lucy's settings and business configuration
- Add and remove team members
- Change roles for other members
- Access billing and payment settings (see [Payment Methods](../billing/payment-methods.md))
- Update the business profile (see [Updating Your Profile](updating-profile.md))
- View and download invoices (see [Invoices and Receipts](../billing/invoices-receipts.md))

The person who created the account is automatically an Admin. You can have multiple Admins.

### Manager

Day-to-day operational access. Managers can:

- View all calls, messages, and appointments
- Manage Lucy's settings (services, hours, responses)
- View team member list
- Access reports and analytics

Managers cannot:

- Add or remove team members
- Change billing or payment settings
- Delete the account

This role is good for an office manager or lead tech who handles scheduling but does not need billing access.

### Viewer

Read-only access. Viewers can:

- View calls, messages, and appointments
- See the dashboard and reports

Viewers cannot:

- Change any settings
- Manage team members
- Access billing

This role works for employees who need to see the schedule or check on incoming calls but should not change configurations. A technician who wants to see their upcoming appointments, for example.

## Changing a Member's Role

Only Admins can change roles:

1. Go to **Settings** and then **Team**
2. Find the team member
3. Click their name or the edit icon
4. Select the new role
5. Click **Save**

The change takes effect immediately. The member's access updates the next time they load a page or refresh their dashboard.

## Removing a Team Member

To remove someone from your account:

1. Go to **Settings** and then **Team**
2. Find the team member
3. Click **Remove** or the delete icon
4. Confirm the removal

Once removed, that person can no longer log in or see any of your business data. Their past actions (if any) remain in the audit trail for your records.

**When to remove someone:**

- An employee leaves your company
- A contractor's engagement ends
- You gave someone temporary access that is no longer needed

## Best Practices

- **Give the least access needed.** If someone only needs to check the schedule, make them a Viewer. You can always upgrade their role later.
- **Have more than one Admin.** If you are the only Admin and you lose access to your account, recovery is harder. Add a trusted partner or manager as a second Admin.
- **Remove former employees promptly.** When someone leaves your business, remove their access the same day. See [Security Settings](security-settings.md) for more on keeping your account safe.
- **Use individual accounts.** Do not share login credentials between people. Each team member should have their own email and password. This keeps your audit trail clean and makes it easy to revoke access for one person without affecting others.

## Common Questions

**Is there a limit on team members?**
Your Atlas UX plan includes team member access at no extra cost. See [Plans and Pricing](../billing/plans-and-pricing.md) for plan details.

**Can a team member see billing information?**
Only Admins can view and manage billing. Managers and Viewers do not have access to payment methods, invoices, or subscription details.

**What happens if I remove the last Admin?**
The system prevents you from removing the last Admin on the account. There must always be at least one Admin.
