# Updating Your Profile

Your profile information shows up in more places than you might think -- caller ID, SMS messages to your customers, invoices, and your public-facing business details. Keeping it accurate means your customers always see the right information.

## What You Can Update

### Business Name

This is the name Lucy uses when she answers calls. When she says "Thank you for calling [your business]," she is pulling from this field. It also appears on:

- SMS confirmations sent to your customers
- Your invoices and billing receipts (see [Invoices and Receipts](../billing/invoices-receipts.md))
- Your Atlas UX dashboard header

To change your business name:

1. Go to **Settings** and then **Profile**
2. Edit the **Business Name** field
3. Click **Save**

Lucy will start using the new name immediately on future calls. Past call logs keep the name that was in use at the time.

### Business Address

Your address is used for invoicing and may be displayed in local business contexts. Update it whenever your business moves or opens a new location.

### Phone Number

This is the phone number associated with your business in Atlas UX. If you need to change the number Lucy answers, contact our support team -- phone number changes involve carrier-level updates and may take a business day to process.

### Email Address

Your account email is used for:

- Logging in
- Password reset emails (see [Password Reset](password-reset.md))
- Billing receipts and notifications
- Important account alerts

To change your email:

1. Go to **Settings** and then **Profile**
2. Update the **Email** field
3. You will receive a verification link at the new email address
4. Click the link to confirm the change

Until you verify the new email, your old one stays active. This prevents someone from locking you out by changing your email without your knowledge.

### Business Logo

Your logo adds a professional touch to your account. Upload it in **Settings** then **Profile**. Accepted formats include PNG, JPG, and SVG. A square image works best -- at least 200x200 pixels.

Your logo may appear on:

- Your Atlas UX dashboard
- Customer-facing communications (as the platform adds more features)

### Business Description

This is the short description of what your business does. Lucy uses this information when answering questions from callers. If a customer calls and asks "What services do you offer?" Lucy pulls from your business description and services list.

Keep it clear and practical. For example:

- "Full-service plumbing for residential and commercial properties in the Dallas area"
- "Hair salon specializing in cuts, color, and extensions, open Tuesday through Saturday"

## How to Update Your Profile

1. Log in to your Atlas UX dashboard
2. Click **Settings** in the navigation
3. Select **Profile**
4. Make your changes
5. Click **Save**

Changes take effect immediately for most fields. Your business name update will be reflected in Lucy's next call. Email changes require verification first.

## What Happens When You Update

- **Business name** -- Lucy uses the new name going forward. Past call records keep the original name.
- **Email** -- Requires verification. Login credentials update after confirmation.
- **Address** -- Future invoices reflect the new address. Past invoices are not retroactively changed.
- **Logo** -- Appears immediately across the dashboard.
- **Description** -- Lucy starts using updated information on her next call.

## Tips

- **Keep your business name consistent** with how customers know you. If you go by "Mike's Plumbing" and not "Michael Thompson Plumbing Services LLC," use the name your customers actually call you.
- **Update your description seasonally** if your services change. Snow removal in winter, lawn care in summer? Update it so Lucy gives accurate answers.
- **Use a professional email** if possible. Customers and your team see this email in various contexts.

## Who Can Update the Profile?

Only users with **admin** access can update profile information. If you need to change something but do not see the option, check your role with your account admin. See [Team Members](team-members.md) for details on roles and permissions.
