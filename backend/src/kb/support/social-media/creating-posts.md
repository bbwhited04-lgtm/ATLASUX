# Creating Social Media Posts

Atlas UX makes social media simple for trade businesses. You don't need to be a marketing expert or spend hours creating content. Tell Atlas what you want to post, and the AI handles the rest -- writing the copy, generating images, and formatting it for each platform.

## How to Create a Post

### Step 1: Open the Content Creator

From your dashboard, go to **Social Media > Create Post**. You'll see a clean editor with a text area and media options.

### Step 2: Write Your Content (or Let AI Do It)

You have two choices:

**Write it yourself** -- Type your post content in the text area. Keep it conversational. Good posts for trade businesses include:
- Before-and-after photos of a job
- A quick tip your customers would find useful
- A seasonal reminder ("Time to schedule your furnace tune-up!")
- A customer testimonial or review highlight
- A team or behind-the-scenes photo

**Use AI assistance** -- Click **Generate with AI** and describe what you want. For example:
- "Write a post about our spring AC maintenance special"
- "Create a tip about preventing frozen pipes"
- "Announce that we're now serving the Westside neighborhood"

Atlas generates polished, professional copy that sounds like you wrote it. You can edit the result before posting.

### Step 3: Add Media (Required)

Every post on Atlas UX must include an image or video. This isn't an arbitrary rule -- posts with media get 2-3x more engagement than text-only posts, and some platforms (Instagram, TikTok, Pinterest) require media to post at all.

You have several options for adding media:

- **Upload your own** -- Photos from job sites, your team, your shop, or your work. Real photos from your business perform best.
- **Choose from your asset library** -- Atlas UX stores media you've uploaded before, so you can reuse your best images.
- **Let Atlas generate an image** -- If you don't have a photo, Atlas will auto-generate a professional image using AI (powered by Flux). It creates something relevant to your post content.
- **Add a video** -- Upload a video clip for platforms like TikTok, YouTube, and Instagram Reels.

See [Media Requirements](media-requirements.md) for supported formats, sizes, and platform-specific details.

### Step 4: Choose Your Platforms

Select which platforms to publish to. Atlas UX supports:

- Instagram
- Facebook
- X (Twitter)
- TikTok
- LinkedIn
- Reddit
- Pinterest
- YouTube
- Threads
- Mastodon
- And more

You can post to one platform or many at once. See [Broadcasting](broadcasting.md) for details on multi-platform posting.

### Step 5: Review or Schedule

After creating your post, you can:

- **Publish now** -- Your post goes out immediately
- **Schedule for later** -- Pick a date and time, and Atlas will publish automatically
- **Save as draft** -- Come back and edit later before publishing (see [Editing Drafts](editing-drafts.md))

## Tips for Better Posts

**Be real.** Customers connect with authentic content. A photo of a messy job site before you cleaned it up is more engaging than a stock photo.

**Keep it short.** Most people scroll quickly. Get to the point in the first sentence or two. Save the details for a follow-up or your website.

**Post consistently.** Even 2-3 posts per week keeps your business visible. Atlas UX makes this easy by generating content ideas and letting you schedule ahead.

**Mix it up.** Alternate between tips, job photos, promotions, and personal moments. Variety keeps your audience interested.

## What Happens Behind the Scenes

When you create a post, Atlas UX:

1. Validates that your content includes media (no text-only posts)
2. Formats the post for each selected platform (character limits, hashtags, aspect ratios)
3. Uploads media to the platform's requirements
4. Publishes through your connected social accounts via Postiz integration

Everything is logged so you can see your posting history and track what's performing.

## Common Questions

**Can I post the same content to all platforms?**
Yes, and Atlas adjusts the formatting for each one. But for best results, consider tweaking the copy for different audiences -- LinkedIn is more professional, TikTok is more casual.

**What if I don't have any photos?**
Atlas auto-generates an image for you. It's not a stock photo -- it's a custom AI-generated image based on your post content. You can also build up your asset library over time by uploading photos from jobs.

**Is there a limit on how many posts I can create?**
Your plan includes a daily posting allowance. You'll see the limit in your dashboard. It's generous enough for most small businesses.
