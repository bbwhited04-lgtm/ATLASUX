# Free Trial Policy

We want you to experience what Lucy can do for your business before you pay a cent. That's why Atlas UX starts with a free trial -- no credit card required, no surprise charges, no strings attached.

## What's Included in the Free Trial

During your trial, you get the full Atlas UX experience. Nothing is locked behind a paywall or limited to tease you. Here's what's available from day one:

- **Lucy, your AI receptionist** -- She answers calls, takes messages, and talks to your customers professionally, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.
- **Appointment booking** -- Lucy can schedule appointments directly into your calendar based on your availability and services.
- **SMS confirmations** -- After booking, your customer gets a text confirming their appointment time and details.
- **Slack notifications** -- Get instant alerts on your phone or computer when Lucy books an appointment, takes a message, or handles a call.
- **Unlimited calls** -- No cap on how many calls Lucy handles during your trial. Whether it's 5 calls or 50, she's ready.
- **Full dashboard access** -- View call logs, appointments, messages, and business settings.

We don't hold anything back. You're trying the real product, not a watered-down demo.

## How Long Does the Trial Last?

Your free trial runs for **14 days** starting from the day you create your account. That gives you two full weeks to set up Lucy, run some test calls, and see real results.

Most of our customers know within the first few days whether Lucy is a fit. But we give you 14 days so there's no pressure.

## No Credit Card Required

When you sign up, we only ask for your name, email, and business info. That's it. We don't ask for a credit card, and we won't auto-charge you when the trial ends. You stay in control.

## What Happens When the Trial Expires

When your 14 days are up, here's what to expect:

- **Lucy stops answering calls.** She won't pick up new calls until you activate a paid plan. Calls will go to your regular voicemail or wherever they went before Lucy.
- **Your data stays safe.** We don't delete your account, settings, call history, or appointments. Everything is right where you left it.
- **You can pick up where you left off.** Choose a plan anytime after your trial expires, and Lucy goes right back to work with all your settings intact. No need to redo the setup wizard.
- **No charges ever hit your card.** Since we didn't collect payment info during signup, there's nothing to charge. Simple as that.

## Extending Your Trial

Need a few more days? Life gets busy, especially when you're running a business. If you didn't get a chance to fully test things out, reach out to our support team. We're happy to extend your trial on a case-by-case basis. We'd rather you feel confident about your decision than rushed.

## Ready to Subscribe?

When you're ready to keep Lucy on the team, head to your dashboard and click **Upgrade** or check out our [plan options](choosing-a-plan.md). The standard plan is $99/month and includes everything you used during the trial -- same features, no surprise tiers.

## Common Questions

**Will I lose my settings if I don't subscribe right away?**
No. Your account and all your configurations are saved. Even if you come back a month later, your setup will be waiting for you.

**Can I cancel during the trial?**
There's nothing to cancel. You didn't enter payment info, so there's no subscription to stop. Just let the trial run out if you decide Atlas UX isn't for you.

**Is the trial really unlimited calls?**
Yes. Lucy handles as many calls as come in during your trial period. No limits.

## What's Next?

Make the most of your trial by following the [Your First Steps After Signing Up](first-steps-after-signup.md) checklist. And when you're ready, see [Choosing a Plan](choosing-a-plan.md) for subscription details.
