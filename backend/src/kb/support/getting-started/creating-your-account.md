# Creating Your Account

Welcome to Atlas UX! In just a few minutes, you'll have your own AI receptionist -- Lucy -- ready to answer calls for your business. Here's how to get started.

## What You'll Need

Before you begin, have these handy:

- **Your email address** -- This becomes your login. Use one you check regularly, since that's where we'll send your verification link and important updates.
- **A password** -- Pick something strong. At least 8 characters with a mix of letters, numbers, and a symbol or two.
- **Your business name** -- The name your customers know you by. You can always change it later.

That's it. No credit card needed to start your free trial.

## Step-by-Step Signup

### 1. Visit the Signup Page

Head to the Atlas UX website and click **Get Started** or **Sign Up**. You'll see a simple form -- no pages and pages of questions.

### 2. Fill In Your Details

Enter your name, email address, and password. Then type in your business name. If you were referred by someone, you can enter a referral code here too.

### 3. Verify Your Email

After you hit **Create Account**, check your inbox for a verification email from Atlas UX. Click the link inside to confirm your address. If you don't see the email within a couple of minutes, check your spam or junk folder.

**Tip:** If the verification link expires, just go back to the login page and request a new one. No need to start over.

### 4. Log In for the First Time

Once your email is verified, log in with the email and password you just created. You'll land right in the onboarding wizard, which walks you through setting up your business and getting Lucy ready to take calls.

## Your Account Dashboard

After completing the onboarding wizard, your dashboard becomes home base. From here you can:

- See calls Lucy has handled
- Check upcoming appointments
- Review messages customers have left
- Manage your business settings

Everything is laid out simply -- no confusing menus or buried settings. If you've ever used a banking app or ordered something online, you'll feel right at home.

## Security and Privacy

We take your information seriously:

- Your password is encrypted and never stored in plain text
- All data is transmitted over secure, encrypted connections
- Your business information is kept private and isolated from other accounts

You can read more in our Privacy Policy, but the short version is: your data is yours, and we protect it.

## Having Trouble?

If you run into any issues during signup:

- **Email not arriving?** Check spam/junk, and make sure you typed your email correctly during signup.
- **Password not accepted?** Make sure it meets the minimum requirements (8 characters, mix of letters and numbers).
- **Page not loading?** Try a different browser or clear your cache.

If none of that helps, reach out to our support team and we'll get you sorted out.

## What's Next?

Once you're logged in, you'll jump straight into the setup wizard. Head over to [Setting Up Your Business with the Onboarding Wizard](setup-wizard.md) to see what comes next.
