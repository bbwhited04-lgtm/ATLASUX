# Getting Help & Contacting Support

When something is not working right or you have a question that the docs do not answer, here is how to get help from the Atlas UX team.

## Before You Contact Support

Taking a few minutes to gather information will help us resolve your issue much faster. Before reaching out:

1. **Check the troubleshooting guides** -- Many common issues are covered here:
   - [Lucy Not Answering Calls](lucy-not-answering.md)
   - [SMS Not Sending](sms-not-sending.md)
   - [Calendar Sync Issues](calendar-sync-issues.md)
   - [Common Error Messages](common-errors.md)

2. **Check the audit log** -- Go to Dashboard > Audit Log and look for recent error entries. The audit trail captures every system action and can reveal exactly what went wrong. See [Audit Trail](../security-privacy/audit-trail.md) for how to read it.

3. **Try the basics** -- Log out and back in, clear your browser cache, and check your internet connection. Sometimes that is all it takes.

## How to Contact Support

**Email:** Send a message to our support team through the Atlas UX dashboard under **Help > Contact Support**, or email us directly.

**In-App Chat:** Look for the help icon in the bottom-right corner of your dashboard for live chat during business hours.

**Response Times:**
- **Critical issues** (Lucy not answering, system down): We aim to respond within 2 hours during business hours.
- **Standard issues** (configuration questions, billing inquiries): Within 1 business day.
- **Feature requests and general questions**: Within 2 business days.

Business hours are Monday through Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM Eastern Time.

## What to Include in a Support Request

The more detail you provide, the faster we can help. Please include:

1. **Your business name** as it appears in your Atlas UX account.
2. **A clear description of the problem** -- What you expected to happen and what actually happened.
3. **Steps to reproduce** -- What were you doing when the issue occurred? Can you make it happen again?
4. **Screenshots or error messages** -- Copy the exact error text or take a screenshot. See [Common Errors](common-errors.md) to check if your error has a known fix.
5. **When it started** -- Did this just begin, or has it been happening for a while? Did anything change recently (new integration, updated settings)?
6. **Device and browser** -- What browser and device are you using? (e.g., Chrome on Windows, Safari on iPhone)

**Example of a good support request:**

> "Lucy stopped answering calls around 3 PM today. The Twilio integration shows Connected in Settings, but test calls go to voicemail. The audit log shows 'ElevenLabs webhook timeout' errors starting at 2:47 PM. I have not changed any settings recently. Using Chrome on Windows 11."

**Example of a request that slows things down:**

> "It's not working."

## Feature Requests

We love hearing what would make Atlas UX more useful for your business. To suggest a feature:

1. Go to **Help > Feature Request** in your dashboard.
2. Describe the feature and how it would help your business.
3. We review all feature requests monthly and prioritize based on demand and impact.

You can also vote on existing feature requests submitted by other users.

## Community Resources

- **Knowledge Base** -- You are reading it right now. Browse the categories in the sidebar for guides on every feature.
- **Release Notes** -- Check the dashboard for announcements about new features and improvements.
- **Status Page** -- If you suspect a system-wide outage, check our status page for real-time updates on service health.

## Account and Billing Help

For billing questions (charges, invoices, plan changes, cancellations):

1. Go to **Settings > Billing > Manage Subscription** to access the Stripe Customer Portal, where you can update payment methods, view invoices, or change plans. See [Stripe Payments](../integrations/stripe-payments.md).
2. For billing questions that the portal does not answer, contact support using the methods above and mention "billing" in your subject line.

## Security Concerns

If you believe your account has been compromised or you have found a security issue:

1. Change your password immediately.
2. Contact support with "SECURITY" in the subject line for priority handling.
3. Review your [Audit Trail](../security-privacy/audit-trail.md) for any unauthorized actions.

See [How We Protect Your Data](../security-privacy/how-we-protect-your-data.md) for our full security overview.
