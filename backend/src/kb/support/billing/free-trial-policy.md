# Free Trial Policy

We want you to see what Lucy can do for your business before you pay anything. That is why Atlas UX offers a free trial with no credit card required to get started.

## What Is Included in the Trial?

Everything. Your free trial gives you the full Atlas UX platform -- the same features paying customers get. Nothing is locked or limited:

- Lucy answers your calls 24/7
- Appointments are booked and confirmed via SMS
- Slack notifications keep you in the loop
- Your dashboard shows every call, message, and booking
- AI agents are available to help with your business operations

We do not give you a watered-down version and hope you imagine how the real thing works. You get the real thing.

## How Long Does the Trial Last?

Your trial gives you enough time to see real results -- actual calls answered, actual appointments booked, actual customers who did not slip through the cracks. The exact duration is shown when you sign up, and we will send you a reminder before it ends so nothing catches you off guard.

## Do I Need a Credit Card?

No. You can sign up and start your trial without entering any payment information. We will not ask for a card until you decide to activate your subscription. No sneaky auto-charges, no "forgot to cancel" billing surprises.

## What Happens When the Trial Ends?

When your trial period is up:

- **Your service pauses.** Lucy stops answering calls, and the platform features become read-only. Nothing breaks -- it just pauses.
- **Your data is kept.** Your business settings, call history, appointments, and messages are all saved. Nothing gets deleted.
- **Easy to activate.** When you are ready to subscribe, just log in, add a payment method, and pick up right where you left off. No re-setup, no starting over.

There is no pressure. We will not call you, chase you, or guilt-trip you into subscribing. If Atlas UX is the right fit, you will know because you saw it work. If it is not, no hard feelings.

## Starting Your Trial

Getting started takes about 10 minutes:

1. **Create your account** -- Head to the signup page and enter your basic info. See [Creating Your Account](../getting-started/creating-your-account.md) for step-by-step instructions.
2. **Run through the setup wizard** -- Tell Lucy about your business, your services, and your hours. Check out [Setting Up with the Onboarding Wizard](../getting-started/setup-wizard.md) for the walkthrough.
3. **Start getting calls answered** -- Once setup is done, Lucy is ready to go.

## After the Trial

If you like what you see, subscribing is straightforward. Atlas UX is $99/mo for the full platform -- see [Plans and Pricing](plans-and-pricing.md) for everything that includes. Add your payment method through [Payment Methods](payment-methods.md) and your service continues without interruption.

If you need more time to decide, reach out to our support team. We are happy to help.

## Common Questions

**Can I extend my trial?**
Reach out to our support team and let us know your situation. We are flexible.

**Will I lose my data if I do not subscribe right away?**
No. Your data is saved after the trial ends. When you activate, everything is right where you left it.

**Can I cancel during the trial?**
There is nothing to cancel -- you have not been charged. If you do not subscribe, your account simply stays paused. See [Cancellation](cancellation.md) for details on canceling an active subscription.
