# Plans and Pricing

Atlas UX keeps billing simple. One plan, one price, everything included. No per-call fees, no hidden charges, no surprises on your invoice.

## The Atlas UX Plan -- $99/mo

For $99 per month, you get the full Atlas UX platform. That includes:

- **Lucy AI Receptionist** -- Answers every call to your business, 24/7/365. She books appointments, sends SMS confirmations, takes messages, and answers questions about your services. See [How Lucy Works](../lucy/how-lucy-works.md) for the full rundown.
- **Unlimited calls** -- There is no cap on how many calls Lucy handles. Whether you get 10 calls a month or 500, the price stays the same.
- **SMS confirmations** -- After booking an appointment, Lucy texts the caller a confirmation with the date, time, and details. No extra cost per message.
- **Appointment booking** -- Lucy books directly into your calendar during the call. No back-and-forth, no phone tag. Learn more in the [Booking Flow](../appointments/booking-flow.md) guide.
- **Slack notifications** -- Get a real-time ping in your Slack channel for every call, message, and booking. Know exactly what is happening without checking a dashboard.
- **Social media management** -- AI agents help you plan and manage your social media presence so your business stays visible online.
- **AI agents** -- A team of specialized AI agents handles different aspects of your business operations behind the scenes, from content creation to customer follow-ups.
- **Dashboard and analytics** -- See your call history, appointment schedule, and customer messages all in one place.

## What You Are Not Paying For

We do not charge per call, per minute, or per SMS. There are no setup fees, no overage charges, and no "premium" tiers that lock features behind a higher price. Every Atlas UX customer gets the same full platform.

Compare that to a part-time receptionist at $15-20/hour, or a traditional answering service that charges per call. Lucy works every hour of every day for a flat $99.

## Who Is This For?

Atlas UX is built for trade and service businesses -- plumbers, HVAC techs, electricians, salons, barber shops, cleaning companies, contractors, and similar businesses. If you miss calls while you are on a job site, under a sink, or cutting someone's hair, Lucy picks up so you do not lose the customer.

## How Billing Works

Your subscription renews monthly on the same date you signed up. We charge the card you have on file through Stripe, our payment processor. You can view your billing history and download invoices anytime from your dashboard. See [Invoices and Receipts](invoices-receipts.md) for details.

If you need to update your payment method, head over to [Payment Methods](payment-methods.md).

## Try It First

Not sure if Atlas UX is right for your business? Start with a free trial -- no credit card required. Lucy will start taking your calls so you can see the difference before you commit. Check out [Free Trial Policy](free-trial-policy.md) for all the details.

## Questions About Pricing?

If you have questions about whether Atlas UX is a good fit for your business, reach out to our support team. We are happy to walk you through everything and make sure it makes sense for your situation before you spend a dime.
