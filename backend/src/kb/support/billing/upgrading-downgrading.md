# Upgrading and Downgrading Your Plan

Right now, Atlas UX offers one plan at $99/mo that includes everything. But as the platform grows, we may introduce additional options. This article explains how plan changes work so you know what to expect.

## Current Plan

Atlas UX currently has a single, all-inclusive plan at $99 per month. Every feature is included -- Lucy AI receptionist, unlimited calls, SMS confirmations, appointment booking, Slack notifications, AI agents, and social media management. See [Plans and Pricing](plans-and-pricing.md) for the full breakdown.

Since there is only one plan today, there is nothing to upgrade or downgrade to. But we are building this platform with trade business owners in mind, and we know one size does not always fit one. If new tiers or add-ons become available, here is how changes will work.

## How Plan Changes Work

### Moving to a Higher Plan

When you upgrade:

- **Changes take effect immediately.** You get access to the new features right away. No waiting until your next billing cycle.
- **Billing is prorated.** You only pay the difference for the remaining days in your current cycle. If you are 15 days into a 30-day cycle and upgrade, you pay half the difference for the rest of that cycle. Your next full invoice reflects the new price.
- **Nothing is disrupted.** Your phone number stays the same, your call history is untouched, your appointments carry over. Lucy keeps answering calls without missing a beat.

### Moving to a Lower Plan

When you downgrade:

- **Changes take effect at the end of your billing cycle.** You keep your current plan's features until the period you already paid for is over. No mid-cycle feature removal.
- **Your data stays intact.** Call history, messages, appointments, business settings -- all of it is kept. If a lower plan has different feature limits, you can still view your existing data.
- **Your phone number is preserved.** Regardless of which plan you are on, your business phone number remains yours.

## How to Change Your Plan

If additional plans become available:

1. Log in to your Atlas UX dashboard
2. Go to **Settings** and then **Billing**
3. You will see your current plan and any available options
4. Select the plan you want and confirm

The system handles the billing math automatically. You will see the prorated amount or credit on your next invoice. Check [Invoices and Receipts](invoices-receipts.md) to review your billing history at any time.

## What About My Data?

Your data is always yours, regardless of what plan you are on:

- **Call history** -- Every call Lucy has handled stays in your dashboard
- **Appointments** -- Past and upcoming bookings remain accessible
- **Messages** -- Customer messages are preserved
- **Business settings** -- Your business name, services, hours, and Lucy's configuration stay the same

We never delete your information because of a plan change.

## Your Phone Number

Your business phone number does not change when you switch plans. Lucy keeps answering the same number, and your customers never know anything changed on the backend.

## Need Help Deciding?

If you are not sure which option is right for your business, our support team can help. We will look at how you are using Atlas UX and recommend the best fit. No upselling, no pressure -- just honest advice.

If you are thinking about pausing or canceling instead, take a look at [Cancellation](cancellation.md) to understand your options.
