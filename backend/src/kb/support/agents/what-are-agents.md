# What Are AI Agents?

Think of AI agents as virtual team members who work for your business around the clock. Each agent has a specific job, just like employees on a real team. One answers your phone calls, another manages your Facebook page, another handles your content calendar. They each have a name, a role, and a set of tools they know how to use.

## Your AI Team, Not a Single Chatbot

Most AI products give you a single chatbot that tries to do everything. Atlas UX works differently. Instead of one tool doing a mediocre job at twenty things, you get a team of specialists who each do one thing well.

For example, Lucy is your receptionist. She answers phone calls, books appointments, takes messages, and sends SMS confirmations to your customers. That is all she does, and she does it 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, in 8 languages. She never calls in sick, never puts a caller on hold, and never forgets to follow up.

Meanwhile, Kelly is managing your X (Twitter) account. Fran is posting to your Facebook page. Timmy is creating TikTok content. They each focus on their lane so nothing falls through the cracks.

## How They Work Together

Your agents do not operate in isolation. They communicate with each other through internal channels, share information, and coordinate tasks. When Lucy takes a call from a potential customer, she can route that lead to the right place. When Binky (your research officer) finishes analyzing market trends, Sunday (your communications lead) turns that research into content the social media agents can publish.

It is like having a full team that already knows how to work together on day one.

## You Stay in Control

This is the part that matters most to business owners. Your agents follow strict safety rules. They can handle routine tasks on their own, like answering a phone call or drafting a social media post. But for anything that costs money or carries risk, they ask for your approval first.

You will never wake up to find an agent ran a $500 ad campaign without your say-so. Every significant action goes through an [approval workflow](../agents/approval-workflows.md) where you review, approve, or reject the proposed action.

For the full breakdown of what agents can and cannot do on their own, see [Agent Permissions](../agents/agent-permissions.md).

## What You Need to Know

- **Agents are always on.** They work nights, weekends, and holidays.
- **Each agent has one specialty.** Lucy answers calls. Kelly runs X. They stay in their lane.
- **They follow your rules.** Daily action limits, spending caps, and approval requirements keep everything under your control.
- **Everything is logged.** Every action every agent takes is recorded in an audit trail so you can see exactly what happened and when.
- **You get notified.** When something important happens, you hear about it through [Slack](../notifications/slack-alerts.md), [email](../notifications/email-notifications.md), or [SMS](../notifications/sms-notifications.md).
