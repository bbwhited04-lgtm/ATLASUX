# Agent Roles — Meet Your AI Team

Atlas UX gives you a full team of AI agents, each with a name and a clear job. Here is who does what.

## The Front Desk

### Lucy — Receptionist

Lucy is your 24/7 professional receptionist. She answers every phone call, books appointments, takes messages, sends SMS confirmations to callers, and notifies you through [Slack](../notifications/slack-alerts.md) whenever something needs your attention. She speaks 8 languages and never misses a call.

Lucy is the first person your customers interact with. She handles the front door so you can stay focused on the job.

## The Social Media Team

Your social media agents work under Sunday, the communications lead. They draft, schedule, and publish content across every major platform. All posts go through an [approval workflow](../agents/approval-workflows.md) before going live.

| Agent | Platform | What They Do |
|-------|----------|-------------|
| **Kelly** | X (Twitter) | Schedules posts, auto-DMs new followers, tracks engagement |
| **Fran** | Facebook | Manages your Page and Groups, publishes posts, monitors comments |
| **Timmy** | TikTok | Creates short-form video content, trend-aligned hooks and captions |
| **Link** | LinkedIn | Professional posts, audience growth, thought leadership content |
| **Dwight** | Threads | Short-form conversational content |
| **Donna** | Reddit | Monitors communities, drafts engagement replies |
| **Cornwall** | Pinterest | Publishes pins, curates boards, drives discovery traffic |
| **Terry** | Tumblr | Formats and publishes content for Tumblr's creative community |
| **Emma** | Alignable | Business networking, connects with local prospects |
| **Reynolds** | Blog | Writes SEO blog posts, cross-posts excerpts to LinkedIn and X |

Think of them as your AI social media team. They keep your business visible on every platform without you having to log into ten different apps.

## Research and Strategy

### Binky — Chief Research Officer

Binky runs daily intelligence cycles. He researches your market, analyzes competitors, and produces briefings that help the team make better decisions. He works with sub-agents like Archy (deep-dive research) and Daily-Intel (daily briefing summaries).

### Mercer — Customer Acquisition Strategist

Mercer designs outreach strategies and analyzes your customer pipeline. He figures out how to get more customers through the door.

## Operations and Support

### Cheryl — Customer Support Specialist

Cheryl handles support tickets, triages incoming requests, and routes them to the right person. She tracks customer feedback and escalates issues when needed.

### Petra — Project Manager

Petra keeps tasks and deliverables organized across the team. She manages sprints and makes sure nothing stalls.

### Claire — Calendar and Scheduling

Claire manages calendars, schedules meetings, prepares agendas, and coordinates invites.

### Sandy — Bookings and Appointments

Sandy manages client-facing appointments, demos, and consultations through the booking system.

## Content and Media

### Sunday — Communications Lead

Sunday is the hub for all written content. She drafts documents, memos, and communications briefs, then routes everything to the right channels. The social media agents report to her.

### Venny — Image and Visual Media

Venny produces branded graphics, images, and visual assets for social posts and marketing materials.

### Victor — Video Production

Victor handles the video pipeline: sourcing media, editing, exporting, and uploading to platforms like YouTube.

## Leadership and Governance

### Atlas — President

Atlas is the execution layer. Every significant action flows through Atlas for final authorization. He writes the audit trail and coordinates the entire team.

### Tina — Chief Financial Officer

Tina manages budgets, cash flow, and financial reporting. She controls spending rules and sends alerts when budgets are at risk.

### Larry — Corporate Secretary

Larry handles compliance, audit integrity, and policy governance. He makes sure the team follows the rules.
