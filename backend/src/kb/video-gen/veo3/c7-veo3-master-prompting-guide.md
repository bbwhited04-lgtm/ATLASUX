## Key Takeaways
- 7-component prompt structure: Subject, Action, Scene, Style, Dialogue, Sounds, Technical (Negative)
- Veo 3 supports direct dialogue with synchronized lip-sync using colon syntax
- Cross-modal reasoning framework integrates text, audio, and visual layers
- Quality assurance validation checklist built into the prompting system
- Duration optimization: 8 seconds is the sweet spot

## The 7-Component Prompt Structure

### 1. Subject
Detailed character/object description with specific physical attributes, clothing, age, build, facial features, ethnicity, hair, eyes, posture, mannerisms, emotional state. Aim for 15+ physical attributes.

### 2. Action
Specific actions, movements, gestures, behaviors, timing, sequence, transitions, micro-expressions, body language, interaction patterns.

### 3. Scene
Detailed environment: location, props, background elements, lighting setup, weather, time of day, architectural details, furniture, decorations.

### 4. Style
Camera shot type, angle, movement, lighting style, visual aesthetic, aspect ratio, film grade, color palette, depth of field, focus techniques.

### 5. Dialogue
Character speech with emotional tone indicators, pacing, volume, accent, speech patterns.
Format: `(Character Name): "Exact dialogue here" (Tone: emotional descriptor/delivery style)`

### 6. Sounds
Specific audio elements: ambient sounds, effects, background audio, music, environmental noise, equipment sounds, natural acoustics.

### 7. Technical (Negative Prompt)
Elements to avoid: subtitles, captions, watermarks, text overlays, unwanted objects, poor quality, artifacts.

## Dialogue Format (Direct Lip-Sync)
```
The teacher stands at the whiteboard and says: "Today we'll explore photosynthesis, the process plants use to convert sunlight into energy."
```

## Cross-Modal Reasoning Framework
Integrate three layers for maximum impact:
- **Visual Layer**: Microscopic details, environments, diagrams
- **Audio Layer**: Narration, ambient sounds, activity sounds
- **Text Layer**: Key terms as visual overlays synchronized with narration

Cross-modal connections:
- Zoom into relevant visuals when narrator mentions specific terms
- Ambient sounds intensify when discussing relevant environments
- Diagrams animate in sync with process explanations
- Text overlays reinforce key concepts without overwhelming visuals

## Commercial Product Shot Template
```
A [product] sits on a rotating pedestal inside a dark studio. High-key rim lighting highlights its contours while a spotlight from above reveals brand details.
Camera: slow 360° dolly-around, 24 fps, 16:9, 1080p.
Audio: cinematic bass hit followed by subtle ambient synth.
Voice-over: "Meet the new [product name]. Designed for those who demand excellence."
```

## Prompt Validation Checklist
- Clear visual description
- Specific camera movements
- Audio specifications included
- Dialogue format (colon syntax)
- Lighting setup defined
- Duration optimization (8 seconds)
- Negative prompts included
- Safety compliance

## Quality Scoring
- Clarity score (1-10)
- Specificity rating
- Technical completeness
- Creative potential
- Risk assessment
