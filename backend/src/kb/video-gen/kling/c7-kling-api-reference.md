## Content

### What is Kling AI?

Kling AI is a new generation of AI creative productivity tools. It is built upon independently developed image generation technology (@Kolors) and video generation technology (@Kling) from Kuaishou's Large Model Algorithm Team. Kling AI offers a wide array of AI-generated images, AI videos, and related controllable editing functionalities. It serves creators (individuals and enterprises) through an online creation platform accessible on web and mobile devices, and developers (individuals and enterprises) by providing API solutions.

### Models Available

#### Kling Video 3.0 Omni

Kling Video 3.0 Omni builds upon its predecessor with native audio-visual synchronization and support for video subject creation. It offers further improved consistency in reference-based tasks, especially for characters and products. The combined capabilities of reference, storyboarding, and audio-visual sync significantly enhance usability. This model also supports scene transitions with up to 6 shots and extends the generation duration up to 15 seconds.

#### Kling Image 3.0 Omni

Kling Image 3.0 Omni enhances the narrative sense in image generation. It introduces new storyboard image set generation, which retains the features of reference images while maintaining scene relevance. This model can directly output 2K/4K ultra-high-definition images and provides further improved detail consistency.

#### Kling Image 3.0

Kling Image 3.0 is designed for high consistency in feature retention and precise response to detail modifications. It offers accurate control over style and tone, coupled with rich imaginative capabilities, allowing for detailed and stylized image generation.

### Kling 3.0 Series API Highlights

The Kling 3.0 Series Models API offers a unified solution for multi-modal input and output. Key highlights include:

- **Superior consistency** across various aspects: subject consistency (supporting cameo, subject with voice control, i2v + subject) and text consistency
- **Enhanced narrative control** allowing for greater freedom, precision, and control over video generation
- **Durations up to 15 seconds** with video scene cuts, ultra-high-definition storyboards/images, and custom seconds
- **Upgraded native audio-visual output** supporting multiple speakers and languages, including different accents
