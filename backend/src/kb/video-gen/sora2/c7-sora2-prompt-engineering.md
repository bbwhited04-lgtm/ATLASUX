## Sora 2 Core Features
- **Synchronized Audio**: Natural sound effects and dialogue generation
- **Enhanced Physics**: Improved realism in motion and object interactions
- **Self-Insertion Cameos**: Ability to insert custom avatars into scenes
- **Advanced Controllability**: More precise camera work and cinematic control

## The Five Pillars Framework
Sora 2 prompts should address five key pillars:
1. **Visual Scene** — Physical environment, lighting, materials, weather
2. **Subject** — Character/object details, clothing, expressions, positioning
3. **Action/Motion** — Movement, timing, physics, interaction patterns
4. **Camera** — Shot type, angle, movement, lens, depth of field
5. **Audio** — Sound effects, dialogue, music, ambient sounds (Sora 2 exclusive)

## World Simulator Paradigm
Sora is not a simple video generator — it's a world simulator. Effective prompts require:
- Physical laws and material properties
- Environmental conditions and atmosphere
- Temporal dynamics and cause-effect relationships
- Spatial reasoning and perspective

## Prompt Categories
- **Hyperrealism**: Photorealistic scenes with precise physics
- **Surreal**: Dreamlike, physics-defying creative content
- **Character-driven**: Story-focused with personality and emotion
- **Cinematic**: Film-quality with specific camera techniques
- **Commercial**: Product showcases and brand content

## Cameo Avatar Integration
Sora 2 supports self-insertion cameos for:
- Custom narration delivery
- Personalized content creation
- End-to-end edited video generation with narration + music + self-insertion
