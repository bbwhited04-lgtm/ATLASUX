# OpenAI Sora 2 Official Announcement and Overview

**Source:** https://openai.com/index/sora-2/
**Author:** OpenAI
**Date:** September 2025

## Key Takeaways

- Sora 2 is OpenAI's second-generation video and audio generation model, a major leap from the original Sora
- Generates professional-quality videos up to 25 seconds long with synchronized dialogue, sound effects, and music from a single text prompt or image reference
- Full HD 1080p resolution is the standard for all Sora 2 generations
- Two model variants: sora-2 (speed/flexibility) and sora-2-pro (cinematic quality)
- Native audio generation -- dialogue, ambience, and sound effects are generated alongside the video, not layered on after
- Physics simulation is dramatically improved -- objects obey Newtonian physics realistically

## Content

### What is Sora 2?

Sora 2 is OpenAI's flagship video and audio generation model, capable of creating richly detailed, dynamic video clips with audio from natural language descriptions or reference images. Built on years of research into multimodal diffusion and trained on diverse visual data, it represents a major evolution from the original Sora model.

### Model Variants

**sora-2 (Standard)**
- Designed for speed and flexibility during the exploration phase
- Generates good quality results quickly
- Well suited for rapid iteration, concepting, and rough cuts
- Supports 4s, 8s, or 12s duration options

**sora-2-pro (Pro)**
- Produces higher quality, more polished and stable results
- Takes longer to render and is more expensive to run
- Best for high-resolution cinematic footage, marketing assets, and situations where visual precision is critical
- Supports 10s, 15s, or 25s durations
- Additional 1792x1024 / 1024x1792 resolution support

### Physics and Realism

Sora 2 understands the 3D geometry and physics of scenes. It does not just generate pixels -- it simulates the world. If a basketball player misses a shot, the ball will rebound off the backboard realistically. If a glass crashes on the floor, Sora 2 calculates the shatter pattern, liquid physics, and reflections consistently. It rarely "hallucinates" impossible physics compared to competitors.

### Video Extensions

You can seamlessly continue any video in Sora with Extensions. Open an existing draft, tap Extend, and describe what should happen next. Sora carries the scene forward while preserving characters, settings, and overall vibe.

### Inserting People and Objects

The model can insert people into Sora-generated environments with an accurate portrayal of appearance and voice. This capability works for any human, animal, or object.

### Availability

As of late 2025, Sora 2 is available in the United States, Canada, Japan, South Korea, Taiwan, Thailand, Vietnam, and several countries in Latin America. Sora 2 Pro is available for users with a $200/month ChatGPT Pro subscription.
