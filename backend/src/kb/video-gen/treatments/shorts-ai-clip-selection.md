## Content

### How AI Clip Selection Works

AI clip selection tools analyze long-form video (podcasts, webinars, livestreams, interviews) and automatically extract the most engaging segments for short-form distribution. The AI evaluates:

1. **Hook strength:** Does the segment start with a compelling statement?
2. **Emotional peaks:** Moments of surprise, humor, insight, or tension
3. **Sentiment shifts:** Changes in tone that indicate key moments
4. **Topic completeness:** Does the clip contain a complete thought/story?
5. **Visual interest:** Speaker energy, gestures, visual changes
6. **Audio analysis:** Voice energy, laughter, emphasis, dramatic pauses
7. **Engagement prediction:** Historical pattern matching against viral content

### Clip Selection Quality Factors

What makes AI clip selection succeed or fail:

**Works well with:**
- Structured content (podcast interviews, tutorials, presentations)
- Clear speaker audio with minimal background noise
- Content with natural segments and topic changes
- Speakers who use emphatic language and clear hooks

**Struggles with:**
- Heavily visual content where audio alone doesn't convey the moment
- Multiple simultaneous speakers / crosstalk
- Highly technical content with specialized vocabulary
- Content where context is required (stories that don't work as excerpts)
- Music-heavy content where lyrics matter

### Best Practices for Source Content

To maximize AI clip selection quality from long-form recordings:

1. **Speak in soundbites:** Train speakers to make standalone statements
2. **Use verbal signposts:** "Here's the key takeaway..." gives AI a hook signal
3. **Vary energy:** Monotone content produces flat clips
4. **Pause between topics:** Clear transitions help AI segment properly
5. **Repeat key points:** Redundancy gives AI more clip candidates to choose from
6. **Keep audio clean:** Minimize background noise, use proper mics
