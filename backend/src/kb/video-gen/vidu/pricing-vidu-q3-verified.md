## Key Takeaways
- Vidu Q3-turbo off-peak at 720P ($0.03/sec) is the cheapest viable video API available

- All tiers support: img2video, text2video, start-end2video
- Duration: 1-16 seconds per generation

## Capabilities (all tiers)
- **img2video** — image as starting frame
- **text2video** — text prompt to video
- **start-end2video** — provide start AND end frames, AI generates the transition (unique to Vidu, perfect for treatments)
- **Duration:** 1-16 seconds
- **Style:** general

## Atlas UX Strategy
- **Default workhorse:** Q3-turbo at 720P off-peak ($0.03/sec)
- **Draft iterations:** Q3-turbo at 540P off-peak ($0.02/sec)
- **Final renders:** Q3-pro at 1080P off-peak ($0.08/sec) — still cheaper than competitors' draft pricing
- **Treatment pipeline:** Use start-end2video for YouTube clip treatments
- **Always schedule off-peak** when possible for 50% savings
