# Vidu Q1 Pricing — Verified from Official Dashboard
**Source:** Vidu official pricing page (screenshot verified 2026-03-18)
**Date Verified:** 2026-03-18
**Notes:** Fixed 5-second duration, flat pricing. Off-peak is 50% of standard.

## Key Takeaways
- Q1 is flat-rate: $0.40 per video standard, $0.20 off-peak — no per-second scaling
- Fixed at 5 seconds and 1080P — no resolution or duration options
- Supports: reference2video, img2video, start-end2video, text2video
- Has **anime** style option (unique to Q1)
- Q1 Classic variant available for all capabilities
- Per-second, Q1 is MORE expensive than Q2-pro-fast ($0.04/sec off-peak vs $0.005/sec)
- Best for: quick 5s clips where simplicity matters, anime-style content

## When to Use Q1
- Quick 5-second bumpers or transitions (flat rate, no math)
- **Anime-style content** — only Q1 has native anime style
- Reference2video when you need 1080P and don't care about duration flexibility
- When simplicity > cost optimization (just pay $0.20 off-peak, done)

## When NOT to Use Q1
- Any clip longer than 5 seconds (Q1 is fixed at 5s)
- Budget-sensitive bulk generation (Q2-pro-fast is 4-8x cheaper per second)
- 720P or 540P is acceptable (Q2/Q3 cheaper tiers available)

## Atlas UX Strategy
- Q1 is a niche tool — use for anime content or quick 5s 1080P bumpers
- For treatments pipeline, Q2-pro-fast is almost always the better choice
- Exception: if Venny/Victor need anime-style output, Q1 is the only option
