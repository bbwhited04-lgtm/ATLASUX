# Sora 2 vs Veo 3 vs Runway Gen-3: 2025 AI Video Model Comparison Guide
**Source:** https://skywork.ai/blog/sora-2-vs-veo-3-vs-runway-gen-3-2025-ai-video-generator-comparison/
**Author:** Skywork AI
**Date:** 2025
**Models Compared:** Sora 2, Veo 3, Runway Gen-3

## Key Takeaways
- Three-way comparison of the leading premium video generation models
- Sora 2 leads for cinematic quality and prompt adherence
- Veo 3 leads for resolution, audio integration, and creative control
- Runway Gen-3 leads for affordability and professional editing tools
- Both Veo 3 and Sora generate native audio; Runway does not
- Pricing ranges from $12/month (Runway) to $249.99/month (Veo full)

## Pros/Cons by Model

### Sora 2
**Pros:**
- Film-grade cinematic quality
- Best physics simulation
- Exceptional prompt adherence for complex scenes
- GPT integration for superior prompt interpretation
- Natural, filmic aesthetic
- Good for shareable content

**Cons:**
- 1080p maximum resolution
- 20-25 second maximum clip length
- $200/month Pro tier
- Limited geographic availability initially

### Veo 3
**Pros:**
- 4K resolution at 60fps
- Up to 2-minute clip length
- Native audio with dialogue, SFX, and music
- Start-frame to end-frame conditioning
- Advertising-grade realism
- Enterprise integration with Google Cloud

**Cons:**
- Expensive ($249.99/month for AI Ultra)
- Google ecosystem dependency
- Slower generation times
- Can feel more "commercial" than "filmic"

### Runway Gen-3
**Pros:**
- Most affordable ($12/month starting)
- Character persistence technology
- Motion brushes for fine-grained control
- Professional editing workflow
- Highest ELO benchmark score

**Cons:**
- No native audio generation
- Costs scale quickly for heavy use ($76+/month)
- Less cinematic than Sora or Veo
- Lower visual quality ceiling
