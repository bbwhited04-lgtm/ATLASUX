# Adobe Firefly Pricing and Plans

## Pricing Overview

Adobe restructured Firefly pricing in early 2026, introducing unlimited standard generations across all paid plans and reserving the credit system for premium features only. This was a significant shift from the previous model where every generation consumed credits.

## Plans

### Free Tier

- **Cost:** $0/month
- **Standard generations:** Limited (approximately 25 per month)
- **Premium credits:** 0
- **Features:** Basic text-to-image, text effects, generative recolor in the Firefly web app
- **Resolution:** Standard (1K)
- **Watermark:** Yes, Content Credentials metadata applied
- **Best for:** Trying Firefly before committing, occasional personal use

### Firefly Standard

- **Cost:** $9.99/month (annual) or $13.99/month (monthly)
- **Standard generations:** Unlimited
- **Premium credits:** 2,000/month
- **Features:** Full Firefly web app, all native Firefly models, reference images, style/structure controls
- **Resolution:** Up to 2K (2048x2048)
- **Commercial use:** Yes, with indemnification
- **Best for:** Freelancers, solo creators, small business owners who need regular AI image generation

### Firefly Pro

- **Cost:** $19.99/month (annual)
- **Standard generations:** Unlimited
- **Premium credits:** 4,000/month
- **Features:** Everything in Standard plus priority generation, higher-quality upscaling, early access to new models
- **Resolution:** Up to 2K
- **Commercial use:** Yes, with indemnification
- **Best for:** Professional designers, content creators with higher volume needs

### Firefly Premium

- **Cost:** $199.99/month
- **Standard generations:** Unlimited
- **Premium credits:** 50,000/month
- **Features:** Everything in Pro plus bulk generation, team management features, dedicated support
- **Resolution:** Up to 2K
- **Commercial use:** Yes, with indemnification
- **Best for:** Agencies, large creative teams, high-volume production environments

## Credit Add-On Plans

Additional premium credits can be purchased separately:

| Add-On Tier | Monthly Cost | Premium Credits |
|-------------|-------------|-----------------|
| 2,000 credits | $9.99/mo | 2,000 |
| 4,000 credits | $19.99/mo | 4,000 |
| 50,000 credits | $199.99/mo | 50,000 |

Credits do not roll over between months. Unused credits expire at the end of each billing cycle.

## What Consumes Premium Credits

### Standard Generations (No Credits on Paid Plans)

- Text-to-image with native Firefly models
- Generative Fill in Photoshop (using Firefly models)
- Generative Expand in Photoshop
- Text effects
- Generative recolor in Illustrator
- Remove tool in Photoshop

### Premium Credit Consumption

| Feature | Credits Per Use |
|---------|----------------|
| Partner models (Google Imagen, OpenAI DALL-E) | 4-8 credits |
| AI video generation (5-second clip) | ~100 credits |
| AI video generation (10-second clip) | ~200 credits |
| Advanced upscaling | 2-4 credits |
| AI translation features | Varies |

## Creative Cloud Bundle Pricing

Firefly capabilities are also included with Creative Cloud subscriptions:

| Plan | Monthly Cost | Included Credits |
|------|-------------|-----------------|
| Single App (e.g., Photoshop) | $22.99/mo | 500 premium credits |
| Photography Plan (Photoshop + Lightroom) | $9.99/mo | 500 premium credits |
| All Apps | $59.99/mo | 1,000 premium credits |
| All Apps + Firefly Pro | ~$79.98/mo | 4,000 premium credits |

Creative Cloud subscribers get Firefly features within their apps (Generative Fill in Photoshop, etc.) plus access to the Firefly web app. The included credits cover premium features; standard generations are unlimited.

## Enterprise Pricing

Adobe offers custom enterprise pricing through Creative Cloud for Enterprise and Creative Cloud for Teams. Enterprise plans include:

- Volume licensing discounts
- Admin console for credit allocation and usage monitoring
- SSO and identity management
- Custom training data options (train Firefly on your brand assets)
- Dedicated account management
- Enhanced indemnification terms
- API access through Firefly Services

Enterprise pricing is quote-based. Contact Adobe sales for specifics.

## Cost-Effectiveness Analysis

### Per-Image Cost (Standard Generations)

On any paid plan, standard Firefly image generations are effectively free (unlimited). This makes Firefly extremely cost-effective for high-volume image generation compared to:

- **Midjourney:** $10-60/month with limited generations (Basic: ~200 images/month)
- **DALL-E 3 via ChatGPT:** $20/month with rate limits
- **Stable Diffusion:** Free to run but requires GPU hardware ($0.50-2.00/hour cloud GPU)

### Per-Image Cost (Premium Features)

At the Standard plan ($9.99/mo for 2,000 credits), premium features cost approximately:
- Partner model image: $0.02-0.04 per image
- 5-second AI video: $0.50 per clip
- 10-second AI video: $1.00 per clip

### Value Proposition

For teams already using Creative Cloud, Firefly's included credits and unlimited standard generations represent significant value since the AI capabilities come at no additional cost beyond existing subscriptions. For standalone use, the $9.99/month Standard plan with unlimited image generation is competitive with any AI image generator on the market.

The main cost risk is premium credit consumption at scale. Teams heavily using AI video or partner models can exhaust credits quickly, and the per-credit cost of add-on plans does not decrease with volume (unlike most SaaS pricing).

## Promotional Periods

Adobe periodically offers unlimited premium generations. From January 23 to March 18, 2026, all Firefly Pro, Premium, and credit add-on subscribers received unlimited generations on all AI image models at up to 2K resolution. Watch for similar promotions during Adobe MAX and other major events.
