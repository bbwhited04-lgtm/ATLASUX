# Pixlr Comprehensive Guide

## What Is Pixlr?

Pixlr is a browser-based photo editing and AI image generation platform developed by Inmagine (formerly 123RF). Founded in 2008 in Sweden, it has evolved from a simple online photo editor into a full-featured AI-powered creative suite. Pixlr requires no software installation — everything runs in the browser, making it one of the most accessible image editing tools available. It serves over 500 million users and is positioned as a lightweight, affordable alternative to Adobe Photoshop and Canva.

## Core Editors

### Pixlr Express (formerly Pixlr X)

Pixlr Express is the quick-edit, beginner-friendly editor. It is designed for fast social media graphics, basic photo touch-ups, and template-based design work. Think of it as the "Canva-like" side of Pixlr. Key capabilities include:

- One-click filters and effects
- Text overlays with a large font library
- Template library for social posts, stories, banners, and ads
- Sticker and element overlays
- Collage maker with grid layouts
- Basic crop, resize, and rotate tools
- Animation support for creating simple motion graphics

### Pixlr Editor (formerly Pixlr E)

Pixlr Editor is the advanced, Photoshop-style editor for users who need layer-based editing, masking, blending modes, and precise control. It supports PSD file import and offers:

- Full layer support with blend modes (multiply, screen, overlay, etc.)
- Advanced selection tools (lasso, magic wand, color range)
- Brush engine with pressure sensitivity support
- Curves, levels, and HSL adjustments
- Clone stamp, heal, and dodge/burn tools
- Non-destructive adjustment layers
- Support for importing PSD, PNG, JPEG, WebP, SVG, and BMP files
- Export at up to 4096x4096 resolution

## AI-Powered Features

Pixlr has aggressively integrated AI across its platform since 2023. The 2025-2026 updates position it as a semi-automated design assistant rather than just a photo editor.

### AI Image Generator (Text-to-Image)

Pixlr's text-to-image generator lets you describe an image in natural language and produces four unique variations per prompt. It offers three generation modes:

- **Fast Mode** — Quick, lower-fidelity generations ideal for brainstorming and concept exploration. Uses fewer AI credits per generation.
- **Pro Mode** — Balanced quality and speed for production-ready images. Good for social media content and marketing materials.
- **Ultra Mode** — Highest quality output with maximum detail and coherence. Best for hero images, print materials, and professional use cases.

Customization options include style presets (Enhance, Anime, Digital Art, Neon Punk, Cinematic, Photographic, Comic Book, Fantasy Art, Line Art, Analog Film), dimension presets (square, wide, tall), color tone adjustments (warm, vibrant, muted, black and white), lighting effects (backlight, dramatic, studio, natural), and negative prompts to exclude unwanted elements.

### AI Background Remover

One of Pixlr's strongest features. The background remover processes images in 3-5 seconds with impressive edge detection, handling complex subjects like hair, fur, and transparent objects better than most free alternatives. It works on images up to 4096x4096 pixels and does not add watermarks, even on the free tier. Batch processing is available for paid users.

### AI Background Changer (AI Backdrop)

Goes beyond simple removal — replaces backgrounds with AI-generated scenes. You describe the desired background environment and the AI composites your subject into the new scene while maintaining consistent lighting and shadows.

### AI Object Remover

Select unwanted objects, people, or blemishes and the AI intelligently fills the area with contextually appropriate content. Similar to Photoshop's Content-Aware Fill but available for free in the browser.

### AI Generative Fill

Paint over any area of an image and describe what you want to appear there. The AI generates content that blends naturally with the surrounding image. Useful for extending images, adding elements, or replacing specific portions.

### AI Generative Transform

Isolate a single object in an image and freely rotate, resize, or reposition it. The AI automatically fills in the exposed background, maintaining scene consistency. This is a unique feature that most competitors do not offer at this price point.

### AI Face Swap

Swap faces between photos with automatic blending for skin tone, lighting, and angle matching. Available without limits on paid plans.

### AI Upscaler

Increase image resolution while preserving detail and sharpness. Useful for rescuing low-resolution images or preparing web images for print.

### AI Cutout

Automatic subject detection and isolation for creating product photos, profile pictures, or design elements with transparent backgrounds. Works on single images or in batch mode.

## Batch Editor

Pixlr's batch processing tool allows paid users to apply edits across multiple images simultaneously. This includes:

- Bulk background removal
- Batch resize and format conversion
- Applying consistent filters or adjustments across a photo set
- Template-based batch design for product catalogs or social media series

## Mobile Apps

Pixlr is available as a mobile app on both iOS and Android. The mobile version includes most of the AI features available in the browser version, including background removal, AI image generation, and filters. The mobile app syncs with your Pixlr account so AI credits are shared across platforms.

## Template and Asset Library

Premium users get access to an extensive library of:

- Design templates for social media, ads, presentations, and print
- Stock photos and illustrations
- Fonts (thousands of premium typefaces)
- Stickers, overlays, and design elements
- Animations and motion presets

## File Format Support

Pixlr handles all standard image formats: JPEG, PNG, WebP, BMP, SVG, and PSD (Photoshop). Export options include quality sliders for JPEG compression and transparency support for PNG. Maximum supported resolution is 4096x4096 pixels.

## Who Is Pixlr For?

Pixlr occupies a sweet spot between Canva (template-focused, no real editing) and Photoshop (professional but expensive and complex). It is ideal for:

- Small business owners who need quick social media graphics and product photos
- Content creators who want AI-assisted editing without a Photoshop subscription
- E-commerce sellers who need batch background removal for product listings
- Marketers who need fast, on-brand visuals without a dedicated designer
- Students and hobbyists who want powerful editing without cost barriers
- Trade businesses that need professional-looking images for websites, flyers, and social posts without hiring a designer

## Platform Availability

- **Browser:** pixlr.com (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge — no installation required)
- **iOS:** App Store (free with in-app purchases)
- **Android:** Google Play Store (free with in-app purchases)
- **Desktop:** No native desktop app — browser-only by design
