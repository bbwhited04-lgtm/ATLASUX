# Pixlr Pricing Guide

## Pricing Tiers Overview

Pixlr uses a freemium model with three paid tiers. AI features run on a credit system — each generation or AI operation consumes credits from your monthly allocation.

| Feature | Free | Plus ($1.99/mo) | Premium ($7.99/mo) | Team ($12.99/mo) |
|---------|------|------------------|---------------------|-------------------|
| Monthly price (billed monthly) | $0 | $1.99 | $7.99 | $12.99 |
| Monthly price (billed annually) | $0 | ~$0.99 | ~$4.90 | ~$9.91 |
| Annual price | $0 | ~$11.88 | ~$58.80 | ~$118.92 |
| AI credits per month | Limited | 80 | 1,000 | 1,000 per seat |
| User seats | 1 | 1 | 1 | Up to 5 |
| Ads | Yes | No | No | No |
| Unlimited saves | No | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| AI Image Generator | Trial only | Yes (80 credits) | Yes (1,000 credits) | Yes (1,000/seat) |
| AI Background Remover | Yes (limited) | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| AI Object Remover | Trial only | Limited | Yes | Yes |
| AI Generative Fill | Trial only | Limited | Yes | Yes |
| AI Generative Transform | No | Limited | Yes | Yes |

## Free Tier Details

The free tier provides access to both Pixlr Express and Pixlr Editor with basic editing tools. AI features are severely restricted:

- **AI Background Remover:** Available but limited to a few uses per day
- **AI Image Generator:** Only during 7-day trial (250 credits, ~75 images)
- **Daily save limit:** Approximately 5 images per day
- **Ads:** Display ads shown throughout the editing interface
- **No batch processing**
- **No private mode** (AI generations may be used for model training)

The free tier is viable for occasional personal use but not practical for any business or regular content creation workflow.

## Plus Plan ($1.99/mo)

The Plus plan is the entry-level paid tier. It removes ads, provides unlimited saves, and grants 80 AI credits per month. At roughly 3-4 credits per AI image generation (depending on mode), this translates to approximately 20-25 AI-generated images per month. Background removal and basic AI tools are included but advanced features like batch processing, private mode, and the full template library are not.

**Best for:** Individuals who want ad-free editing and occasional AI use. Not enough credits for regular content creation.

## Premium Plan ($7.99/mo)

The Premium plan is the sweet spot for most users. With 1,000 AI credits per month, you can generate approximately 250-330 images (Fast mode) or 100-150 images (Ultra mode) per month. Full access to all AI tools, the complete template and asset library, batch processing, and private AI mode (your generations are not used for training).

**Cost per AI generation:**
- Fast mode: ~$0.02-0.03 per image
- Pro mode: ~$0.04-0.06 per image
- Ultra mode: ~$0.05-0.08 per image

**Best for:** Small businesses, content creators, and marketers who need regular AI-assisted image creation and editing. The annual plan at ~$4.90/month is excellent value.

## Team Plan ($12.99/mo)

The Team plan includes up to 5 user seats, each with their own 1,000 AI credits per month (5,000 total credits across the team). Adds team management tools for assigning roles and managing access. All Premium features are included.

**Best for:** Small agencies, marketing teams, or businesses with multiple people creating visual content.

## AI Credit Consumption Rates

Different AI operations consume credits at different rates:

| Operation | Approximate Credit Cost |
|-----------|------------------------|
| Image Generation (Fast) | 3 credits |
| Image Generation (Pro) | 5 credits |
| Image Generation (Ultra) | 7-10 credits |
| Background Removal | 1 credit |
| Object Removal | 2 credits |
| Generative Fill | 3-5 credits |
| AI Upscale | 2-3 credits |
| Face Swap | 3 credits |

Note: Credit costs are approximate and may vary. Pixlr occasionally adjusts credit consumption rates.

## Free Trial

New users get a 7-day free trial of the Premium plan with 250 AI credits. This is enough to test the AI image generator (approximately 75 images in Fast mode) and evaluate whether the platform meets your needs. No credit card is required to start the trial.
