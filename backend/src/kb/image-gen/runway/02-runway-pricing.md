# Runway ML Pricing

## Subscription Plans

### Free Plan — $0/month
- 125 one-time credits (non-renewable)
- 3 projects maximum
- Watermarked outputs
- Gen-4 Image and Gen-4 Video access
- Community support
- Best for: trying the platform before committing

### Standard Plan — $12/month
- 625 credits per month
- Unlimited projects
- No watermarks on outputs
- 4K upscaling available
- 100 GB asset storage
- Best for: individuals exploring AI-assisted creative work

### Pro Plan — $28/month
- 2,250 credits per month
- All Standard features
- Higher resolution outputs
- Priority generation queue
- Best for: regular creators and small teams producing consistent content

### Unlimited Plan — $76/month
- Unlimited video generations (credits not consumed for video)
- 2,250 credits for non-video features (images, upscaling, etc.)
- All Pro features
- Best for: heavy video users who also generate images

### Enterprise — Custom Pricing
- All Pro features
- SSO and compliance tools
- Workspace analytics
- Custom annual credit pools shared across team members
- Dedicated account management
- Annual contracts with negotiated credit volumes
- Contact sales@runwayml.com for pricing

## Credit System

Runway uses a credit-based consumption model. Credits are the universal currency across all generation types.

**Important credit rules:**
- Credits do not roll over — unused credits expire at the end of each billing cycle
- Additional credits can be purchased a la carte within any paid plan
- Enterprise credits refresh annually rather than monthly

## Video Generation Credit Costs (for comparison)

| Model | Credits per Second |
|-------|--------------------|
| Gen-4 Turbo | 5 credits/sec |
| Gen-4 / Gen-3 Alpha | 10-12 credits/sec |
| 4K Upscaling | 2 credits/sec |

A 10-second Gen-4 video costs 100-120 credits — equivalent to 20-24 standard images or 50-60 Turbo images. Video burns credits significantly faster than image generation.

## API Pricing

API access is available through Runway's developer portal at dev.runwayml.com.

- Credits cost $0.01 each when purchased through the API portal
- Up to $9,999 in credits can be purchased in-app
- Larger volumes require contacting the sales team
- API credits are separate from subscription plan credits
- No monthly subscription required for API-only usage — pay as you go

## Value Assessment

Runway's image generation pricing is competitive when using the Turbo model ($0.02/image), but the standard Gen-4 Image at $0.05-$0.08 per image is more expensive than DALL-E 3 or Stable Diffusion API alternatives. The real value proposition is the integrated pipeline — if you need both images and video, Runway eliminates the need to pay for and manage multiple platforms.

For pure image generation volume, the Pro plan at $28/month offering 450 standard images or 1,125 Turbo images per month is the sweet spot for most professional users.
