# Prompt Templates by Industry

Ready-to-use prompt templates for trade businesses. Each template is designed to produce professional marketing images when used on any major AI image generation platform. Customize the bracketed variables to match your specific business.

## How to Use These Templates

1. **Copy the template** for your industry and use case
2. **Replace bracketed variables** with your specific details
3. **Add a negative prompt** from the Negative Prompting Guide if your platform supports it
4. **Set the aspect ratio** for your target platform (1:1 for Instagram, 16:9 for website, 9:16 for Stories)
5. **Generate 3-5 variations** and select the best result

### Recommended Negative Prompt (use with all templates)

For platforms that support negative prompts (Stable Diffusion, FLUX, Leonardo AI):
```
blurry, low quality, watermark, text, cartoon, anime, deformed hands,
extra fingers, bad anatomy, ugly, dark, stock photo, clip art
```

## Cross-Industry Usage Notes

### Adapting Templates Across Platforms

These templates are written in descriptive natural language that works on any platform. For platform-specific optimization:

- **Midjourney:** Condense to key descriptors, add `--ar 16:9 --v 6 --style raw` as needed
- **DALL-E 3:** Use as-is — DALL-E understands natural language well
- **Stable Diffusion / FLUX:** Convert to comma-separated tokens for best results
- **Ideogram:** Add quoted text for any text you want in the image

See the Cross-Platform Prompt Translation Guide (05) for detailed conversion methods.

### Building Your Own Templates

Use these as starting points. After generating with a template, save the exact prompt that produced your best result. Over time, your custom template library will be more effective than these generic versions because it will be tuned to your specific platform, model version, and aesthetic preferences.
