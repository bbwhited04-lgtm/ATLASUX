# Style Modifiers Reference

This is a comprehensive reference of style modifiers that work across AI image generation platforms. Use these as building blocks when constructing prompts. Each modifier is tested across multiple models and categorized by type.

## Photography Styles

### Editorial Photography
High-quality, magazine-ready images with intentional composition and professional lighting. The most versatile style for trade business marketing.
- **Modifier:** `editorial photography`, `magazine editorial`, `editorial style`
- **Produces:** Polished, story-driven images with strong composition
- **Best for:** Website hero images, social media feature posts, proposals

### Product Photography
Clean, focused images that showcase an object or installation. Neutral backgrounds, even lighting, sharp detail.
- **Modifier:** `product photography`, `commercial product shot`, `catalog photography`
- **Produces:** Object-focused images with minimal distracting backgrounds
- **Best for:** Equipment showcases, tool displays, fixture presentations

### Real Estate / Architectural Photography
Wide-angle interior and exterior shots with corrected verticals, even lighting, and emphasis on space and design.
- **Modifier:** `real estate photography`, `architectural photography`, `interior design photography`
- **Produces:** Spacious, well-lit interior and exterior images
- **Best for:** Before/after renovations, completed project showcases, room transformations

### Portrait Photography
Subject-focused with background separation. Flattering lighting and natural skin tones.
- **Modifier:** `portrait photography`, `professional headshot`, `environmental portrait`
- **Produces:** People-focused images with blurred backgrounds
- **Best for:** Team member photos, customer testimonials, "about us" page

### Documentary / Photojournalism
Candid, authentic, slightly raw. Captures real moments rather than posed setups.
- **Modifier:** `documentary photography`, `photojournalistic style`, `candid photography`
- **Produces:** Authentic, unpolished images that feel real
- **Best for:** Behind-the-scenes content, work-in-progress shots, social media authenticity

### Macro Photography
Extreme close-up showing fine detail invisible to the naked eye.
- **Modifier:** `macro photography`, `extreme close-up`, `micro detail`
- **Produces:** Detailed close-ups of textures, materials, connections
- **Best for:** Craftsmanship details, material quality, before/after detail comparisons

### Aerial / Drone Photography
Elevated perspective showing properties, neighborhoods, or large-scale projects from above.
- **Modifier:** `aerial photography`, `drone shot`, `bird's eye view photography`
- **Produces:** Overhead or elevated angle views
- **Best for:** Roofing, landscaping, large commercial projects, property overviews

### Street Photography
Urban, candid, often high contrast with interesting geometry and human elements.
- **Modifier:** `street photography`, `urban photography`
- **Produces:** Dynamic urban scenes with candid energy
- **Best for:** Service vehicles in urban settings, storefront images

### Lifestyle Photography
Staged but natural-looking scenes of people living and working. Common in advertising.
- **Modifier:** `lifestyle photography`, `lifestyle advertising`, `aspirational lifestyle`
- **Produces:** People interacting naturally in appealing environments
- **Best for:** Social media ads, customer experience visualization

### Flat Lay Photography
Top-down view of objects arranged on a flat surface. Popular on social media.
- **Modifier:** `flat lay photography`, `overhead flat lay`, `knolling`
- **Produces:** Organized top-down arrangements of tools, materials, products
- **Best for:** Tool showcases, service package visualizations, social media posts

## Art and Illustration Styles

### Digital Art / Digital Illustration
Clean, modern illustrations with precise lines and vibrant colors. Scalable and versatile.
- **Modifier:** `digital illustration`, `digital art`, `modern digital illustration`
- **Best for:** Social media graphics, blog post headers, infographic elements

### Watercolor
Soft, organic, hand-painted look with visible brush strokes and translucent color layering.
- **Modifier:** `watercolor painting`, `watercolor illustration`, `loose watercolor`
- **Best for:** Elegant invitations, seasonal greetings, soft brand imagery

### Oil Painting
Rich, textured, traditional painting style with deep colors and visible brush work.
- **Modifier:** `oil painting`, `classic oil painting`, `impasto oil painting`
- **Best for:** Prestigious/luxury brand imagery, fine art feel

### Vector Illustration
Flat, geometric, clean-edged graphics. The style of logos, icons, and modern design.
- **Modifier:** `vector illustration`, `flat vector style`, `vector graphic`
- **Best for:** Logo concepts, icon sets, simple brand graphics

### Pixel Art
Retro, grid-based digital art reminiscent of early video games.
- **Modifier:** `pixel art`, `16-bit pixel art`, `retro pixel art`
- **Best for:** Playful social media content, gaming-adjacent branding

### Anime / Manga
Japanese animation and comic style with characteristic features.
- **Modifier:** `anime style`, `manga illustration`, `Studio Ghibli style`
- **Best for:** Niche social media content, younger audience targeting

### Comic Book / Graphic Novel
Bold lines, dramatic shading, panel-style composition.
- **Modifier:** `comic book style`, `graphic novel illustration`, `pop art comic`
- **Best for:** Social media storytelling, action-oriented service marketing

### Isometric Illustration
3D-like technical illustrations from a fixed 30-degree angle. Popular for explainers and diagrams.
- **Modifier:** `isometric illustration`, `isometric view`, `isometric 3D style`
- **Best for:** Service process explanations, system diagrams, how-it-works graphics

### Line Drawing / Sketch
Minimal, hand-drawn look with clean or rough lines and minimal color.
- **Modifier:** `line drawing`, `pencil sketch`, `architectural line drawing`, `blueprint sketch`
- **Best for:** Planning visuals, conceptual designs, elegant minimal branding

### Infographic Style
Data-visualization-focused with icons, charts, and text-heavy layouts.
- **Modifier:** `infographic style`, `data visualization`, `information design`
- **Best for:** Educational content, stat-driven social posts (note: text rendering limits apply)

### 3D Render
Computer-generated three-dimensional imagery with controlled lighting and materials.
- **Modifier:** `3D render`, `Cinema 4D render`, `Octane render`, `Blender 3D`
- **Best for:** Product mockups, architectural visualization, modern tech aesthetic

## Lighting Modifiers

### Natural Light

| Modifier | Effect | Best For |
|----------|--------|----------|
| `golden hour lighting` | Warm, low-angle, long shadows | Exterior shots, romantic/warm mood |
| `blue hour lighting` | Cool, twilight, subtle colors | Evening exterior shots |
| `overcast natural light` | Soft, even, no harsh shadows | Interiors, products, portraits |
| `bright midday sun` | High contrast, strong shadows | Energetic outdoor scenes |
| `dappled sunlight` | Light filtering through leaves/objects | Garden, outdoor casual scenes |
| `window light` | Directional, soft, single-source | Interior scenes, portraits |
| `backlit` | Subject silhouetted or rim-lit | Dramatic effect, mood shots |
| `open shade` | Even lighting in shaded outdoor area | Even skin tones, soft look |

### Studio Light

| Modifier | Effect | Best For |
|----------|--------|----------|
| `softbox lighting` | Even, diffused, professional | Product photography, headshots |
| `ring light` | Flat, even facial lighting | Beauty, tutorials, close-ups |
| `three-point lighting` | Balanced key/fill/back | Standard professional setup |
| `Rembrandt lighting` | Dramatic triangle on cheek | Character portraits |
| `butterfly lighting` | Shadow under nose, glamorous | Beauty photography |
| `split lighting` | Half face lit, half in shadow | Dramatic effect |
| `strip lighting` | Narrow, edge-defining | Product edges, silhouettes |

### Atmospheric Light

| Modifier | Effect | Best For |
|----------|--------|----------|
| `neon lighting` | Colorful, urban, electric | Nightlife, modern, tech |
| `candlelight` | Warm, intimate, flickering | Cozy interiors |
| `fluorescent lighting` | Cool, flat, institutional | Industrial, office settings |
| `warm LED recessed lighting` | Modern residential glow | Contemporary interiors |
| `volumetric lighting` | Visible light rays/beams | Dramatic, atmospheric |
| `ambient lighting` | Soft, omnidirectional, no strong shadows | Calm, even scenes |
| `chiaroscuro` | Extreme light/dark contrast | Fine art, dramatic |
| `moonlight` | Cool, blue-tinted, dim | Nighttime exteriors |

## Composition Modifiers

### Camera Position

| Modifier | Effect |
|----------|--------|
| `eye level` | Natural, relatable perspective |
| `low angle / worm's eye view` | Subject appears powerful, imposing |
| `high angle` | Subject appears smaller, shows layout |
| `bird's eye view / top-down` | Directly overhead |
| `Dutch angle / tilted` | Dynamic, unsettling energy |
| `over-the-shoulder` | Intimate, following perspective |
| `point of view / first person` | Viewer's perspective |

### Framing and Layout

| Modifier | Effect |
|----------|--------|
| `rule of thirds` | Subject off-center, dynamic |
| `centered composition` | Subject in middle, formal |
| `symmetrical composition` | Mirrored balance |
| `leading lines` | Architectural/environmental lines guide eye |
| `negative space` | Large empty areas (great for adding text) |
| `frame within a frame` | Subject seen through doorway/window |
| `fill the frame` | Subject takes up entire image |
| `foreground interest` | Object in front creates depth |
| `layered composition` | Distinct foreground, middle, background |

### Depth

| Modifier | Effect |
|----------|--------|
| `shallow depth of field / bokeh` | Background blur, subject isolation |
| `deep depth of field` | Everything sharp, front to back |
| `tilt-shift` | Selective focus, miniature effect |
| `rack focus` | Focus transition (implied in still images) |
| `soft focus` | Slightly dreamy, romantic |

### Lens Effects

| Modifier | Effect |
|----------|--------|
| `wide angle lens / 16mm` | Expansive, distortion at edges |
| `standard lens / 50mm` | Natural perspective |
| `telephoto / 200mm` | Compressed perspective, isolation |
| `fisheye` | Extreme wide angle, barrel distortion |
| `anamorphic lens` | Cinematic, horizontal flares |
| `tilt-shift lens` | Corrected verticals for architecture |
| `lens flare` | Light artifacts from strong source |

| `monochromatic` | Single color in varying shades |
| `complementary colors` | Opposing colors for contrast |
| `analogous colors` | Adjacent colors for harmony |

### Tone and Processing

| Modifier | Effect |
|----------|--------|
| `high contrast` | Deep blacks, bright whites |
| `low contrast` | Flat, even, subtle |
| `HDR` | Extreme dynamic range, vivid |

| `black and white` | Monochrome grayscale |
| `sepia` | Warm brownish vintage tone |

## Quick-Reference Combinations for Trade Businesses

### The "Professional Service" Stack
```
editorial photography, soft professional lighting, eye level composition,
shallow depth of field, warm and professional mood, clean modern setting
```

### The "Completed Project" Stack
```
real estate photography, bright natural window lighting, wide shot with
deep depth of field, clean and inviting atmosphere, warm whites and
natural materials
```

### The "Social Media Engagement" Stack
```
lifestyle photography, golden hour lighting, rule of thirds composition,
vibrant saturated colors, bright and optimistic mood
```

### The "Before Image" Stack
```
documentary photography, flat fluorescent lighting, slightly messy
environment, muted desaturated tones, eye level straight-on composition
```

### The "Hero Image" Stack
```
editorial photography, dramatic volumetric lighting, wide angle lens,
cinematic color grading, bold and professional mood, centered composition
with leading lines
```
