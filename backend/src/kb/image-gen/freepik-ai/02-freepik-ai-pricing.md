# Freepik AI Pricing

## Subscription Plans

Freepik offers a tiered subscription model where each tier unlocks progressively more AI credits and platform features. All plans are priced per user per month, with discounts for annual billing.

### Free Tier

Freepik provides limited free access to AI image generation through Pikaso. Free users can generate a small number of images per day using the Classic model. Generations are watermarked and come with restrictive licensing (attribution required, limited commercial use). Free access is useful for testing the platform but not viable for production content.

### Essential Plan --- $5.75/month (billed annually)

The entry-level paid plan provides access to Freepik's AI tools only. This plan does not include downloads from the stock library. It is designed for users who want AI generation capabilities without the broader asset library. Credit allocation is modest and suited for occasional use.

### Premium Plan --- $12.00/month (billed annually)

The most popular plan for individuals and small teams. Premium includes:

- Full access to Freepik's stock library (vectors, photos, PSD files, icons)
- AI image generation via Pikaso with standard monthly credit allocation
- Commercial licensing for both stock downloads and AI-generated images
- Access to Flux and Mystic models

This is the best value tier for most business users who need both stock assets and AI generation.

### Premium+ Plan --- $24.50/month (billed annually)

The enhanced tier adds significantly more AI credits and premium features:

- **45,000 monthly AI credits** (or 600,000 annual credits)
- Unlimited standard image generation on select models
- Access to advanced AI tools including the Magnific upscaler
- AI video generation capabilities
- Priority generation queue
- Ability to purchase additional credit packs

Premium+ is designed for marketing teams and content creators with high-volume generation needs.

### Pro Plan --- $158.33/month (billed annually)

The enterprise-grade tier for agencies and large teams:

- **300,000 monthly AI credits** (or 4,000,000 annual credits)
- All Premium+ features
- Extended commercial licensing
- Priority support
- Suitable for multi-user teams and high-volume production workflows

## Credit System Explained

Freepik uses an internal credit currency for AI operations. Credits are consumed differently depending on the tool and model:

| Operation | Credit Cost |
|-----------|------------|
| Classic model (fast, basic quality) | 1 credit per image |
| Flux 1.1 Pro (standard quality) | ~50 credits per image |
| Mystic 2.5 (high quality, 2K) | ~100-200 credits per image |
| AI Assistant (GPT-4o powered) | Up to 500 credits per interaction |
| AI Video (short, standard) | 150-500 credits |
| AI Video (HD, longer) | 1,000-2,800 credits |
| Image upscaling (Magnific) | Varies, premium credits |

Credits refresh monthly on subscription plans. Premium+ and Pro users can purchase additional credit packs. Extra purchased credits expire 3 years after purchase and are consumed only after monthly credits are depleted.

## Effective Cost Per Image

Based on the credit allocations and per-image costs:

- **Classic model on Premium**: Effectively fractions of a cent per image
- **Flux on Premium+**: Approximately $0.02-0.05 per image (45,000 credits / ~50 credits per image = ~900 images/month at $24.50)
- **Mystic on Premium+**: Approximately $0.05-0.12 per image depending on resolution and settings
- **Video generation**: $0.50-3.00+ per clip, making it significantly more expensive than image generation

For comparison, individual stock photo downloads on traditional platforms typically cost $1-10 per image, making Freepik's AI generation dramatically cheaper at volume.

## API Pricing

Freepik offers a developer API with pay-as-you-go pricing separate from the consumer subscription plans.

**Key API pricing characteristics:**
- No upfront payment required
- No mandatory subscription --- pure usage-based billing
- Transparent per-request pricing
- Covers image generation, stock search, and AI editing endpoints

Specific API endpoint pricing is available at `freepik.com/api/pricing`. The API is suited for developers integrating Freepik's generation capabilities into their own platforms and requires a separate API key obtained through the developer portal at `docs.freepik.com`.

## Comparison to Pure Stock Subscriptions

| Platform | Monthly Cost | What You Get |
|----------|-------------|-------------|
| Freepik Premium | $12/mo | Stock library + AI generation |
| Shutterstock Standard | $29/mo | 10 stock downloads, no AI gen |
| Adobe Stock | $29.99/mo | 10 stock downloads + Firefly |
| Getty Images | $49.99/mo | 5 stock downloads, no AI gen |
| iStock | $12.99/mo | 10 stock downloads, no AI gen |

Freepik's bundled approach offers significantly better value than traditional stock-only subscriptions, particularly for users who can supplement stock downloads with AI-generated alternatives.

## Commercial Licensing Terms

All images generated on paid Freepik plans include commercial licensing rights:

- Generated images can be used in commercial projects, marketing materials, websites, and social media
- No attribution required on paid plans
- Standard limitations apply: no resale of generated images as standalone stock, no use in trademark applications without modification
- Free tier generations require attribution and have more restrictive commercial terms
- API-generated images follow the same licensing terms as the corresponding plan tier

The commercial license is one of Freepik's key advantages --- unlike some AI generators where commercial rights are ambiguous or require higher-tier plans, Freepik includes commercial use from the Premium tier onward.
