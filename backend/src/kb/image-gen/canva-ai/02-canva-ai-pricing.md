# Canva AI — Pricing and Credits

## Plan Tiers

### Canva Free

- **Cost:** $0
- **AI allowance:** Limited — approximately 50 lifetime uses of premium AI features (Magic Media, Magic Write)
- **Magic Media:** Available but severely restricted; once the 50-use lifetime cap is reached, AI generation is no longer available
- **Queue times:** 8-20 minutes during peak hours (early 2026 reports)
- **Background Remover:** Not available
- **Brand Kit:** Not available
- **Best for:** Testing Canva AI before committing; occasional one-off use

### Canva Pro

- **Cost:** $15/month or $120/year (per person)
- **AI allowance:** ~500 premium AI uses per month (pooled across all AI features)
- **Magic Media:** Full access to image, graphic, and video generation
- **Background Remover:** Included
- **Brand Kit:** Included (one brand kit)
- **Magic Edit, Eraser, Expand, Grab:** All included
- **Best for:** Solo creators, freelancers, small business owners

### Canva Business (replaced Canva Teams for new signups)

- **Cost:** $20/seat/month
- **Minimum:** No published minimum, but pricing is per-seat
- **AI allowance:** ~500 premium AI uses per person per month
- **Brand Kit:** Multiple brand kits
- **Team features:** Shared templates, brand controls, approval workflows
- **Note:** Canva Teams ($10/seat/month, 3-user minimum) is no longer available for new signups — existing subscribers are grandfathered
- **Best for:** Marketing teams, agencies, multi-person operations

### Canva Enterprise

- **Cost:** Custom quote only
- **Median contract:** ~$13,319/year (based on verified purchase data)
- **AI allowance:** Custom — negotiated based on team size and usage
- **Additional features:** SSO, advanced admin controls, dedicated support, custom integrations, compliance features
- **Best for:** Large organizations with governance and compliance requirements

## AI Credit System

Canva uses a **pooled monthly AI allowance** rather than a per-image credit system. Key details:

- All premium AI features (Magic Media, Magic Edit, Magic Eraser, Magic Expand, Magic Write, Magic Design) draw from the same monthly pool
- Image generation is among the most AI-intensive actions and consumes more of the allowance than simpler tasks like Magic Write
- The exact credit cost per generation is not publicly disclosed — Canva abstracts this behind the ~500 uses/month figure
- As of early 2026, Canva added a real-time usage tracker so users can monitor consumption mid-month
- When the monthly limit is reached, users experience "short pauses between generations" rather than a hard cutoff — they can continue by re-entering prompts, but the experience is throttled
- Allowances reset monthly on the billing date

## Estimated Cost Per Image

Since Canva bundles AI credits into the subscription, the effective cost per image depends on usage:

| Scenario | Monthly Cost | AI Images/Month | Effective Cost/Image |
|----------|-------------|-----------------|---------------------|
| Pro, light AI use | $15 | ~50 images | ~$0.30 |
| Pro, heavy AI use | $15 | ~200 images | ~$0.075 |
| Pro, maxed out | $15 | ~500 images | ~$0.03 |
| Business, per seat | $20 | ~500 images | ~$0.04 |
| Free tier | $0 | ~50 lifetime | $0 (then unavailable) |

**Important caveat:** The 500-use figure is shared across all AI features. If a user also uses Magic Write, Magic Edit, and other AI tools, fewer uses are available for image generation.

## Bulk Generation Economics

Canva is cost-effective for low-to-moderate AI image generation when users also need the design platform. However, for bulk generation (100+ images/month focused solely on AI generation), standalone tools or API-based generators offer better value:

- **Canva Pro at $15/month** makes sense when the user needs both design tools AND occasional AI generation
- **For 500+ images/month**, API-based generators (DALL-E API, Flux API, Replicate) are dramatically cheaper at $0.01-0.05 per image with no caps
- **For quality-sensitive work**, Midjourney Standard at $30/month provides superior output with unlimited relax-mode generations

## Key Takeaway

Canva AI's pricing is not competitive as a standalone image generator. Its value is in the bundled design workflow — generate, edit, template, brand, resize, export, and publish all in one platform. Users paying for Canva Pro are paying for the design platform first and getting AI generation as a bonus feature. For Atlas UX agent automation, the lack of AI generation API makes Canva unsuitable for programmatic image creation workflows.
