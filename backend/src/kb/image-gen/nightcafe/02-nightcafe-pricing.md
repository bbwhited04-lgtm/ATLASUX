# NightCafe Studio — Pricing and Credit System

## Plan Overview

NightCafe offers a free tier plus four paid subscription plans. All paid plans grant "PRO" status, which unlocks unlimited base generations on curated models, faster generation speeds, and additional platform features.

| Plan | Monthly Price | Quarterly Price (per mo) | Credits/Month | Key Benefit |
|------|--------------|--------------------------|---------------|-------------|
| **Free** | $0 | — | 5/day (earned) | Unlimited base Stable Diffusion, daily challenge credits |
| **AI Beginner** | $5.99 | $4.79 | 100 | PRO status, unlimited base generations |
| **AI Hobbyist** | $9.99 | $7.99 | 200 | Good for regular creators |
| **AI Enthusiast** | $19.99 | $15.99 | 500 | Heavy creative use |
| **AI Artist** | $49.99 | $39.99 | 1,400 | Power users and serious artists |

Annual billing is also available at further discounted rates.

## Free Tier Details

NightCafe's free tier is genuinely usable, not just a trial:

- **5 free credits per day** awarded on login or app usage
- **3 additional credits per day** for entering and voting in the daily challenge
- **10 bonus credits** every 5-day activity streak
- **Unlimited base Stable Diffusion generations** at no credit cost
- **Free community FLUX model access** — base generations on 3,000+ fine-tuned FLUX models cost zero credits (single image, short runtime)
- No credit card required to sign up

A consistently active free user can earn roughly 8 credits per day (5 daily + 3 challenge), plus streak bonuses. This is enough for casual experimentation but not sustained high-volume generation.

## Credit Types

### Fast Credits

Fast Credits are included with paid subscriptions. They generate images at full speed with no queue delay and work on any model or configuration. Monthly Fast Credits refresh each billing cycle and do not roll over.

### Relax Credits

Relax Credits are earned through free daily activities — logging in, entering challenges, earning badges, and streak rewards. They function identically to Fast Credits but are processed at lower server priority during busy periods. In practice, this means a wait of a few seconds to approximately one minute. Relax Credits provide a way for free users to generate beyond the base free models.

## Credit Cost Per Generation

Credit costs vary based on several factors:

| Factor | Impact on Credit Cost |
|--------|----------------------|
| **Model selection** | More advanced models (DALL-E 3, FLUX Pro) cost more credits than base Stable Diffusion |
| **Resolution** | Higher resolutions consume more credits |
| **Advanced settings** | ControlNet, start images, inpainting, and other modifiers increase credit cost |
| **Number of images** | Generating multiple images per prompt multiplies cost |
| **Runtime** | Longer generation runtimes (more steps/iterations) increase cost |

### Approximate Credit Costs by Model

- **Base Stable Diffusion** — Free for PRO users (unlimited base generations); minimal credits for free users
- **SDXL** — 1-4 credits per generation depending on resolution and settings
- **DALL-E 3** — Typically 2-5 credits per generation
- **FLUX** — Base community models free (single image, short runtime); advanced FLUX generations 2-6 credits
- **Neural Style Transfer** — 1-3 credits depending on resolution
- **VQGAN+CLIP** — 1-2 credits for standard configurations

Note: Exact per-generation costs fluctuate as NightCafe adjusts pricing. The platform's pricing page at creator.nightcafe.studio/pricing shows current rates.

## PRO Benefits Beyond Credits

All paid plans (and bulk credit purchases) unlock:

- **Unlimited base generations** on curated AI models (no credits deducted)
- **Faster generation speeds** via Fast Credits
- **Priority server access** during high-traffic periods
- **Advanced features** including enhanced upscaling and additional model configurations
- **No watermarks** on generated images
- **Higher resolution options** for output images

## Cost Analysis: Credits vs. Competitors

### Light Use (10-20 images/week)
The free tier handles this comfortably. NightCafe is one of the most generous free options available.

### Moderate Use (50-100 images/week)
The AI Hobbyist ($9.99/mo) or AI Enthusiast ($19.99/mo) plans cover this range, especially with unlimited base generations supplementing credit usage. Comparable to Midjourney's Basic ($10/mo) and Standard ($30/mo) plans.

### Heavy Use (200+ images/week)
The AI Artist plan ($49.99/mo, 1,400 credits) is competitive, but credits can deplete quickly with advanced settings. At this volume, dedicated API access (Stability AI, Replicate) may offer better per-image economics for production workflows.

### Production/Commercial Scale
NightCafe is not designed for this. No API, no batch processing, no programmatic access. At commercial scale, the credit system becomes a bottleneck and cost liability.

## Seasonal Promotions

NightCafe runs periodic promotions including Black Friday (historically 50% off PRO plans), annual plan offers with significant discounts, and occasional unlimited model-specific offers (e.g., unlimited FLUX generation promotions). These can substantially reduce effective cost for users willing to commit to longer billing cycles.
