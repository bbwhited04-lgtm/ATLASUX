# Fotor AI - Prompting Guide with Examples

## Prompt Structure

Fotor's AI image generator responds best to structured prompts following this formula:

**Subject + Detailed Description + Environment + Art Style + Lighting + Image Quality**

The platform supports negative prompts to exclude unwanted elements (e.g., "no text, no watermark, no blurry"). Style presets can be selected from the UI to supplement your text prompt, and similarity controls let you dial in how closely the output should match a reference image when using image-to-image mode.

## 10 Example Prompts

### 1. Profile Photo Enhancement

**Use case:** Transform a casual photo into a polished professional headshot.

**Prompt:** "Professional corporate headshot of a confident business person, clean neutral background, soft studio lighting, sharp focus on face, business attire, warm natural skin tones, high resolution portrait photography"

**Style preset:** Photorealistic
**Negative prompt:** "blurry, distorted face, cartoon, illustration, harsh shadows"

**Tips:** For best results with Fotor's AI headshot feature, upload an existing selfie and let the AI handle the transformation. The dedicated headshot generator often outperforms the general text-to-image tool for this specific use case.

### 2. Social Media Graphic

**Use case:** Create an eye-catching Instagram post background for a fitness brand.

**Prompt:** "Vibrant gradient abstract background with flowing organic shapes in coral pink and electric blue, modern minimalist design, smooth color transitions, social media aesthetic, clean composition with space for text overlay"

**Style preset:** Digital Art
**Negative prompt:** "text, words, letters, cluttered, busy pattern"

**Tips:** Generate the background image, then use Fotor's template editor to add text, logos, and brand elements on top. This two-step workflow produces more polished results than trying to include text in the AI prompt.

### 3. Product Listing Photo

**Use case:** Create a clean product showcase for an e-commerce listing.

**Prompt:** "Minimalist product photography setup, clean white marble surface, soft diffused natural window lighting from the left, shallow depth of field, premium luxury aesthetic, commercial photography style, 4K quality"

**Style preset:** Photorealistic
**Negative prompt:** "cluttered background, harsh shadows, dark, low quality"

**Tips:** For actual product photos, Fotor's background remover is more practical — photograph your real product, remove the background, then place it on an AI-generated or template background. This gives you the real product with an idealized setting.

### 4. Blog Header Image

**Use case:** Generate a hero image for a blog post about sustainable technology.

**Prompt:** "Futuristic green city with solar panels and wind turbines integrated into modern architecture, lush rooftop gardens, clean energy aesthetic, bright optimistic mood, aerial perspective, digital illustration style, warm golden hour lighting"

**Style preset:** Digital Art
**Negative prompt:** "dark, dystopian, pollution, ugly, low resolution"

**Tips:** Blog images work best at landscape orientation. Generate at the highest resolution available and crop to your blog's header dimensions (typically 1200x630 or 1920x600). Fotor's resize tool can handle the final adjustment.

### 5. Avatar Creation

**Use case:** Create a stylized avatar for gaming profiles or social accounts.

**Prompt:** "Stylized character portrait in semi-realistic anime style, vibrant hair color, detailed eyes with light reflections, confident expression, cosmic space background with nebula, dynamic lighting with rim light effect, digital painting quality"

**Style preset:** Anime
**Negative prompt:** "ugly, deformed, extra fingers, bad anatomy, blurry eyes"

**Tips:** Fotor's anime AI art generator has dedicated style presets (Disney, Makoto Shinkai, Miyazaki) that produce more consistent results than describing the style in the prompt. Select the style preset and keep your prompt focused on the subject details.

### 6. Background Replacement

**Use case:** Replace a plain office background with a more interesting setting for a video call screenshot or portrait.

**Prompt:** "Modern coworking space interior with large windows overlooking a city skyline at sunset, warm ambient lighting, contemporary furniture, indoor plants, bokeh effect on background, professional office environment"

**Style preset:** Photorealistic
**Negative prompt:** "blurry, distorted, cartoon, illustration, fisheye"

**Tips:** The most effective workflow is: (1) use Fotor's background remover on your original photo, (2) generate the new background separately, (3) composite them using Fotor's editor. This preserves your authentic appearance while placing you in the new environment.

### 7. Photo Restoration Prompt

**Use case:** Restore and enhance a damaged vintage family photograph.

**Prompt:** Not applicable for direct generation. Use Fotor's dedicated Photo Restoration tool.

**Workflow:** Upload the damaged photograph to Fotor's AI Photo Restoration feature. The AI will automatically detect and repair scratches, tears, fading, color degradation, and noise. Follow up with the AI Photo Enhancer to upscale resolution and sharpen details. If the original is black and white, apply the Photo Colorize feature to add realistic color.

**Tips:** Process in order: restoration first (repair damage), then enhancement (upscale quality), then colorization (add color). Each step builds on the previous one for best results.

### 8. Collage with AI Elements

**Use case:** Create a mood board or collage mixing real photos with AI-generated elements.

**Prompt (for AI elements):** "Decorative floral border elements, watercolor style roses and eucalyptus leaves, transparent background, soft pastel colors, botanical illustration, delicate and elegant"

**Style preset:** Watercolor / Illustration
**Negative prompt:** "photorealistic, harsh colors, neon, dark"

**Tips:** Generate individual decorative elements, then use Fotor's collage maker to combine them with your real photographs. The template library includes pre-designed collage layouts that handle spacing and arrangement automatically.

### 9. Seasonal Promotional Image

**Use case:** Create a holiday promotional banner for a small business.

**Prompt:** "Cozy winter holiday scene with warm golden string lights, pine branches, cinnamon sticks, and red berries on a rustic wooden table, festive bokeh background, commercial photography style, inviting warm atmosphere, space for text on the right side"

**Style preset:** Photorealistic
**Negative prompt:** "text, words, logos, Santa Claus, cartoon, cluttered"

**Tips:** Leave intentional negative space in the composition for promotional text overlay. Specify "space for text on [side]" in your prompt. After generation, use Fotor's design editor to add your sale details, pricing, and branding.

### 10. Headshot Enhancement

**Use case:** Improve an existing professional photo with AI enhancement.

**Prompt:** Not applicable for direct generation. Use Fotor's AI Photo Enhancer and Skin Retouch tools.

**Workflow:** Upload the existing headshot to Fotor. Apply AI Photo Enhancer to improve resolution and clarity. Use AI Skin Retouch for natural-looking skin smoothing without an artificial appearance. Adjust lighting and color balance using the manual editing tools. Export at the highest resolution available.

**Tips:** Fotor's retouch tool is calibrated for natural results — it smooths skin texture without creating the plastic look common in over-processed photos. For LinkedIn and corporate use, subtle enhancement is more effective than dramatic changes.

## General Prompting Tips for Fotor

1. **Be specific about style** — Fotor's style presets are powerful. Select one that matches your intent rather than describing style entirely in text.
2. **Use negative prompts** — Exclude common artifacts like "blurry, distorted, extra fingers, low quality" to improve consistency.
3. **Keep text out of AI generation** — AI-generated text is unreliable. Generate the image first, add text in Fotor's editor.
4. **Specify lighting** — "soft studio lighting," "golden hour," "dramatic side lighting" dramatically affect mood and quality.
5. **Include quality markers** — Terms like "high resolution," "4K," "professional photography," and "detailed" nudge the model toward higher quality output.
6. **Iterate** — Generate multiple variations and refine your prompt based on what works. Fotor allows batch generation to explore options quickly.
