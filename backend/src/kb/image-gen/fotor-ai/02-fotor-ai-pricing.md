# Fotor AI - Pricing and Plans

## Plan Overview

Fotor offers a freemium model with two paid tiers, plus a standalone AI credit purchase system for users who need generation capacity beyond their subscription allocation.

## Free Tier

- Access to basic photo editing tools (crop, rotate, filters, adjustments)
- Limited template library
- 8 free AI credits on signup
- Earn additional credits through daily check-ins
- Basic AI image generation with watermark-free output
- Limited access to AI enhancement tools
- Standard resolution output
- Fotor branding on some exports

The free tier is sufficient for occasional use and trying out features, but active users will quickly exhaust the credit allocation.

## Fotor Pro - $8.99/month

- **Monthly billing:** $8.99/month
- **Annual billing:** $39.99/year ($3.33/month effective)
- All free tier features plus:
  - Advanced AI editing tools (beauty touch-up, AI enlarger, object remover)
  - AI background remover with full-resolution output
  - AI photo enhancer with HD upscaling
  - HDR (High Dynamic Range) processing
  - Auto-resized designs for multiple platform formats
  - Expanded template library
  - Increased AI credit allocation
  - Priority processing for AI tasks
  - No ads
  - Batch processing capabilities
  - Cloud storage for projects

Fotor Pro is the best value for small business owners and regular content creators who need reliable access to AI editing tools without the full stock photo library.

## Fotor Pro+ - $19.99/month

- **Monthly billing:** $19.99/month
- **Annual billing:** $89.99/year ($7.49/month effective)
- Everything in Fotor Pro plus:
  - 1,000,000+ HD stock photos for personal and commercial use
  - Weekly updates on all stock resources
  - Largest AI credit allocation
  - Premium templates and design assets
  - Commercial licensing for all generated and edited content
  - Priority customer support

Fotor Pro+ is designed for agencies, marketing teams, and heavy content producers who need the stock photo library alongside AI generation capabilities.

## AI Credit System

Fotor uses a credit-based system for AI features. Credits are consumed each time you use an AI generation or enhancement tool. The exact credit cost varies by feature — image generation typically costs more credits than simple enhancements.

### Standalone Credit Purchases

For users who need more credits than their subscription provides, Fotor offers standalone credit packages:

| Credits | Price | Per Credit |
|---------|-------|------------|
| 1 | $0.12 | $0.120 |
| 100 | $8.00 | $0.080 |
| 500 | $30.00 | $0.060 |
| 1,000 | $50.00 | $0.050 |
| 5,000 | $200.00 | $0.040 |
| 10,000 | $280.00 | $0.028 |

### Subscription Credit Rates

- **Monthly subscription plans:** approximately $0.05 per credit
- **Annual subscription plans:** approximately $0.015 per credit

Annual billing provides the steepest credit discount, making it the most cost-effective option for consistent users.

## Cost Comparison

At the per-credit rates, Fotor's AI image generation falls in the budget-friendly category compared to dedicated generators:

- **Fotor (annual Pro):** ~$0.015-0.05 per credit (exact images-per-credit varies by feature)
- **Midjourney Basic:** $10/month for ~200 generations ($0.05/image)
- **DALL-E 3:** ~$0.04-0.08 per image via API
- **Leonardo.ai Free:** 150 tokens/day (free but limited)

Fotor's value proposition is strongest when you factor in the full editing suite, templates, and enhancement tools bundled alongside generation — you are paying for an entire creative platform, not just an image generator.

## Enterprise and API Pricing

Fotor offers custom enterprise pricing for organizations needing:
- Volume licensing for teams
- API access (via Fotor OpenAPI)
- SDK integration
- Dedicated support
- Custom credit allocations

Enterprise pricing is available by contacting Fotor's sales team directly. API pricing operates on a token consumption model rather than traditional rate limits.

## Refund Policy

Fotor subscriptions are billed in advance. Annual plans typically do not offer prorated refunds, though Fotor's support team may accommodate cancellation requests on a case-by-case basis. Always check the current terms of service before purchasing.
