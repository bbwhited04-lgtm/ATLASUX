## Recommendations by Budget

### "I want free"
Use **Ideogram** (40 imgs/day) for text-heavy work, **Recraft** (50 credits/day) for general and vector work, **Leonardo** (150 tokens/day) for game/character assets.

### "I want cheap but good" (under $0.03/image)
Use **Google Imagen 4 Fast** at $0.02/img (or $0.01 batch). Best quality-to-cost ratio with a real API. For even cheaper, **GPT Image 1 Mini** at $0.005/img is adequate for thumbnails and previews.

### "I want the best value" ($0.03-0.05/image)
Use **FLUX.2 Pro via fal.ai** at $0.03/img or **GPT Image 1.5** at $0.04/img. Both deliver top-tier quality at prices that scale well to thousands of images.

### "I want the absolute best quality"
Use **Google Imagen 4 Ultra** at $0.06/img. Highest quality score (9.5) at a price that's still reasonable. Add upscale for $0.003 to get 4K output at $0.063 total.

### "I want the best for a subscription"
Use **Ideogram Pro** at $20/mo for 12,000 images (effective $0.002/img) or **Adobe Firefly Standard** at $9.99/mo for unlimited standard generations. Both are extraordinary value if volume matches your needs.

## Atlas UX Cost Optimization Strategy

For a platform serving trade businesses at $99/mo subscription:

**Image generation budget per tenant:** Target $1-3/mo in image costs (1-3% of subscription revenue).

| Scenario | Images/Tenant/Mo | Provider | Cost/Tenant/Mo | Margin Impact |
|----------|-----------------|----------|----------------|---------------|
| Light use | 20 | GPT Image 1 Mini | $0.10 | 0.1% |
| Medium use | 50 | Google Imagen 4 Fast | $1.00 | 1.0% |
| Heavy use | 100 | FLUX.2 Pro (fal.ai) | $3.00 | 3.0% |
| Heavy use (optimized) | 100 | Google Imagen 4 Fast Batch | $1.00 | 1.0% |

**Strategy:**
1. Default to Google Imagen 4 Fast Batch for async work ($0.01/img)
2. Use FLUX.2 Pro via fal.ai for real-time requests ($0.03/img)
3. Set per-tenant monthly image cap (e.g., 100 images) to control costs
4. Use GPT Image 1 Mini ($0.005/img) for preview/thumbnail generation
5. At 1,000 tenants, total image cost: $1,000-3,000/mo (well within margins)
