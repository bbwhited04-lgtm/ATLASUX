## Detailed Analysis by Category

### 1. Skin and Human Features

The most revealing test of photorealism — how naturally human skin, hair, eyes, and expressions are rendered.

| Rank | Model | Skin Texture | Hair Detail | Eye Realism | Expression | Hands/Fingers |
|------|-------|-------------|------------|-------------|------------|---------------|
| 1 | Imagen 4 Ultra | 9.8 | 9.5 | 9.7 | 9.5 | 9.0 |
| 2 | GPT Image 1.5 | 9.5 | 9.3 | 9.5 | 9.3 | 8.5 |
| 3 | FLUX.2 Max | 9.5 | 9.4 | 9.3 | 9.0 | 8.0 |
| 4 | Seedream V4.5 | 9.3 | 9.0 | 9.0 | 8.8 | 8.5 |
| 5 | Midjourney v6.1 | 9.0 | 9.2 | 9.0 | 8.5 | 7.5 |

**Insights:**
- **Imagen 4 Ultra** produces pore-level skin detail with accurate subsurface scattering, making portraits nearly indistinguishable from photographs.
- **FLUX.2 Max** handles diverse skin tones with exceptional accuracy but occasionally produces extra fingers.
- **Midjourney** tends toward idealized/stylized beauty, which reads as "too perfect" rather than naturally photorealistic. Hands remain a weakness.
- **Seedream V4.5** from ByteDance is the dark horse — excellent skin textures rivaling FLUX.2 at a fraction of the cost.

## Where Each Model Falls Apart

Every model has failure modes. Understanding these is critical for production use.

| Model | Common Failures | Frequency |
|-------|----------------|-----------|
| **Imagen 4 Ultra** | Over-strict safety filters rejecting legitimate prompts; occasionally "too clean" (no film grain/imperfection) | Low |
| **GPT Image 1.5** | Sometimes adds unwanted stylization to photos; can "overthink" simple prompts | Low |
| **FLUX.2 Max** | Extra fingers/hands; occasional anatomical anomalies in complex poses | Medium |
| **Seedream V4.5** | Less reliable with Western architecture and cultural contexts; occasional color banding | Medium |
| **Midjourney v6.1** | Text rendering is unreliable; hands/fingers still fail ~15% of time; tendency to over-stylize | Medium |
| **FLUX.2 Pro** | Slight softness compared to Max; occasional texture repetition in large areas | Low-Medium |
| **DALL-E 3 (HD)** | "Painted" look on skin; hair looks synthetic at close inspection; limited pose variety | Medium-High |
| **Stable Image Ultra** | Skin looks waxy at times; inconsistent quality between generations; limited prompt understanding | Medium |
| **Leonardo Phoenix** | Obvious AI artifacts in backgrounds; skin rendering is noticeably synthetic | Medium-High |
| **Adobe Firefly 3** | Flat lighting; images look like stock photos; low dynamic range; conservative compositions | High |

## Practical Recommendations

### "I need the most photorealistic image possible"
Use **Google Imagen 4 Ultra** ($0.06/img). Generate, then upscale to 4K for $0.003 more. Total: $0.063 for a 4K photorealistic image that rivals professional photography.

### "I need photorealism on a budget"
Use **Google Imagen 4 Fast** ($0.02/img, or $0.01 batch). Quality score 7.8 for photorealism at a fraction of Ultra's cost. 80% of the quality for 33% of the price.

### "I need photorealism with API automation"
Use **FLUX.2 Pro via fal.ai** ($0.03/img). Best balance of photorealism, cost, and API accessibility. Or Google Imagen 4 Fast via Vertex AI ($0.02/img).

### "I need photorealism with text in the image"
Use **GPT Image 1.5** ($0.04/img) for the best combination of photorealism and text accuracy. Or generate the image with Imagen 4/FLUX.2 and overlay text programmatically.

### "I need photorealism for free"
Use **Recraft** (free tier, 50 credits/day) or **Ideogram** (free tier, 40 images/day). Both produce decent photorealistic output at no cost, though a tier below the top models.

### For Atlas UX trade business use cases
Trade businesses (plumbers, HVAC, salons) need photorealistic images for:
- Before/after renovation shots: **FLUX.2 Pro** ($0.03/img)
- Team/staff portraits: **Google Imagen 4 Fast** ($0.02/img)
- Job site documentation mockups: **GPT Image 1 Mini** ($0.005/img) — adequate for internal use
- Marketing hero images: **Midjourney** (manual, for highest quality) or **FLUX.2 Max** ($0.06/img, automated)
