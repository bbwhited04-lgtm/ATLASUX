## Recommendations by Creative Need

### "I want the most beautiful output"
**Midjourney v6.1** — The aesthetic quality of Midjourney's output is unmatched. Every image looks like a professional photographer or artist created it. Worth the $10-30/mo subscription for anyone doing creative work.

### "I want creative control"
**GPT Image 1.5** ($0.04/img) — Best prompt adherence means you get what you describe. Ideal when you have a specific creative vision and need the AI to execute it precisely.

### "I want to explore a wide range of styles"
**SDXL with LoRAs** (self-hosted or via API) — The open-source ecosystem has LoRAs for virtually every art style, period, and medium. Requires technical knowledge but offers unmatched flexibility.

### "I want professional illustration"
**Recraft V3** ($0.04/img raster, $0.08/img vector) — Clean, professional illustration output with true vector/SVG capabilities. Best for design work that needs to scale.

### "I want game-ready creative assets"
**Leonardo.ai** (~$0.008/img subscription) — Purpose-built for game development with character consistency, multiple art style models, and ControlNet support.

### "I want creative art for free"
**Ideogram** (40 imgs/day free) for typography-art fusion, **Leonardo.ai** (150 tokens/day free) for game/character art, **Recraft** (50 credits/day free) for illustration.

### For Atlas UX creative content
Trade business marketing benefits from:
- **Eye-catching social media visuals:** FLUX.2 Pro via fal.ai ($0.03/img) for vibrant, scroll-stopping imagery
- **Professional service illustrations:** Recraft V3 ($0.04/img) for clean, professional iconography and service descriptions
- **Seasonal/promotional graphics:** Ideogram via fal.ai ($0.05/img) for text-integrated promotional graphics with readable sale prices, phone numbers, and CTAs
