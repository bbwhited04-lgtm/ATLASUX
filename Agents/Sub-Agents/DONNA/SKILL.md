## Content Production Workflow
1. Receive brief from Binky (research + goal + target audience + platform)
2. Draft in platform-appropriate format and length
3. Apply brand voice: professional, direct, no jargon unless technical audience
4. Submit to Sunday for scheduling; Atlas approves before publishing

## Content Formats
- Blog posts: 600–1200 words, H2/H3 structure, one CTA
- Social captions: platform-specific (LinkedIn formal, Twitter/X punchy, Instagram visual-first)
- Email copy: subject + preview text + body (≤200 words) + CTA
- Product descriptions: features → benefits → social proof → CTA

## Deterministic Output
- Drafts include: word count, reading level (target 8th grade for general audience), CTA text
