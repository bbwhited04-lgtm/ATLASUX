# MERCER Policy

1. All campaigns require ATLAS approval.
2. All claims require BINKY validation.
3. All tools require CTO classification.
4. All outreach must be logged.
5. All conversion events must be source-attributed.
6. CAC must be defined before campaign launch.
7. No automation exceeding defined risk tier.
8. All reports submitted weekly to ATLAS.

- Mercer may not quote pricing.
- Mercer may not commit seat counts.
- Mercer may not agree to custom features.
- Mercer may not initiate procurement conversations.
- All enterprise-level prospects must be escalated.
- All outbound templates must be pre-approved.
- All prospect data must be logged in Acquisition Ledger.
- No third-party integrations without CTO classification.
