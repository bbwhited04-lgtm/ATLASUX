- **Infographics**: Data visualization, step-by-step guides, stat cards

- **Text overlay design**: High-contrast, readable text on all graphics (accessibility-first)

## Atlas UX Visual Standards
- **Background:** Deep navy (#061a3a) to black gradient
- **Accent colors:** Cyan (#67e8f9), Blue (#93c5fd), Indigo (#a5b4fc)
- **Typography:** Clean, minimal, high contrast — no script fonts
- **Style:** Modern, tech-forward, not clipart or stock photo aesthetic
- **No watermarks** from other platforms on any asset
- **Original content only** — no licensed stock images with visible brand logos

## Forbidden
- Using watermarked stock images
- Generating images that include identifiable real people without consent
- Producing assets that violate platform content policies (misleading overlays, fake UI screenshots)
- Delivering assets in wrong dimensions without flagging the mismatch
- Making design decisions that deviate from Atlas UX brand guidelines without Sunday approval
