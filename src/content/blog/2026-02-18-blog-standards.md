## Writing rules

- Prefer **short paragraphs**.
- Avoid jargon.
- State uncertainty plainly.

## Safety rules

- No medical/legal/financial advice presented as certainty.
- No dark patterns.
- No hidden subscriptions.

## SEO rules

- Always include an excerpt.
- Use categories and tags.
- Cross-link to at least two other posts when possible.
