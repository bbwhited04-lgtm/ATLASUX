# Solo Founder → Large-Cap Company: Complete Scaling Framework

## The Journey in Stages

### Stage 0: Solo Founder (1 person)

```
YOU ARE: Idea stage. Everything is you.
PRIORITIES:
□ Validate problem (talk to 50+ potential users)
□ Build MVP (or no-code prototype)
□ Incorporate company (Private Limited India, C-Corp Delaware US)
□ Open business bank account
□ Domain, social handles, basic branding
□ Startup India DPIIT registration (India — for tax benefits)
DEPARTMENTS THAT EXIST: You = CEO + CTO + PM + Designer + Support + Sales
GOVERNANCE: None needed. You make all decisions.
```

### Stage 1: Founding Team (2-5 people)

```
YOU ARE: Building v1. First hires are co-founders or early employees.
PRIORITIES:
□ Ship product to first users
□ Product-market fit validation (do users come back without being asked?)
□ ESOP pool created (10-15%)
□ Basic financial tracking (Zoho Books / spreadsheet)
□ First paying customer
NEW HIRES: 1-2 engineers, maybe 1 designer (or outsource)
DEPARTMENTS: Engineering (informal), Everything else (founder)
GOVERNANCE: Informal. Founder decides. Daily alignment over coffee/Slack.
```

### Stage 2: Seed Stage (5-15 people)

```
YOU ARE: Raised seed funding. Building the core team.
PRIORITIES:
□ Achieve product-market fit
□ Hire functional leads (engineering lead, first PM, first marketer)
□ Basic processes: Sprint planning, weekly standup, monthly review
□ Customer support system (not just founder's inbox)
□ Basic analytics (know your DAU, retention, revenue)
□ First SLA with customers (if B2B)
NEW DEPARTMENTS:
  ├── Engineering (3-5 people)
  ├── Product (1-2 people: PM + Designer)
  ├── Marketing (1 person or outsourced)
  └── Support (1 person or founder handles)
GOVERNANCE: Weekly team meeting. Founder makes major decisions. Informal board (investors + advisor).
NEW COMPLIANCE: GST registration, TDS, basic employment compliance
```

### Stage 3: Growth Stage — Series A (15-40 people)

```
YOU ARE: Product-market fit achieved. Time to scale.
PRIORITIES:
□ Scale engineering team (separate frontend, backend, mobile, QA)
□ Hire department heads (Head of Engineering, Head of Product, Head of Marketing)
□ Formalize HR: Offer letters, policies, POSH committee (mandatory >10 employees India)
□ Financial controls: Expense policy, approval workflows, monthly close
□ Security baseline: SOC 2 preparation, pen testing, access controls
□ Board meetings quarterly (formal agenda, minutes, resolutions)
□ OKRs introduced
NEW DEPARTMENTS:
  ├── Engineering (8-15)
  ├── Product & Design (3-5)
  ├── Marketing & Growth (3-5)
  ├── Sales (if B2B) (2-5)
  ├── Operations & Support (3-5)
  ├── Finance (1-2: accountant + controller)
  └── HR (1 person or outsourced)
GOVERNANCE: Board (4-5 members). Monthly leadership sync. Quarterly all-hands.
NEW COMPLIANCE: Employee PF registration, shop & establishment license, professional tax
```

### Stage 4: Scale-Up — Series B/C (40-150 people)

```
YOU ARE: Market leader in your niche. Scaling aggressively.
PRIORITIES:
□ VP/Director-level hires for each function
□ Middle management layer (team leads → managers → directors)
□ Engineering: Platform team, DevOps/SRE, data engineering
□ Internal tools and automation (reduce manual ops)
□ International expansion preparation (if applicable)
□ SOC 2 Type II certification
□ Internal audit function (or outsourced)
□ L&D program formalized
□ Employer branding investment
□ PR agency retained
NEW DEPARTMENTS:
  All previous PLUS:
  ├── Data & Analytics (3-5)
  ├── DevOps/SRE (2-3)
  ├── Legal (1 in-house + outside counsel)
  ├── People/HR (2-3)
  ├── Internal IT (1-2)
  ├── Business Development (2-3)
  └── Customer Success (separate from Support) (3-5)
GOVERNANCE: Board (5-7). Audit committee formed. Monthly exec team meeting. Quarterly board.
NEW COMPLIANCE: Transfer pricing (if international), ESOP trust, insider trading policy draft
```

### Stage 5: Pre-IPO (150-500+ people)

```
YOU ARE: Preparing for public markets or large-cap private operation.
PRIORITIES:
□ Board reconstitution (majority independent directors)
□ All board committees formed (Audit, NRC, Stakeholder, Risk)
□ Company Secretary appointed
□ 3-year financial audit cleanup (Ind AS / IFRS compliant)
□ Internal controls over financial reporting formalized
□ Corporate governance code compliance
□ D&O insurance
□ IPO advisory team engaged (BRLM, legal, auditors)
□ Investor relations function established
□ CSR policy (if applicable by threshold)
□ Whistleblower mechanism
□ Bug bounty program
NEW DEPARTMENTS:
  All previous PLUS:
  ├── Investor Relations (1-2)
  ├── Corporate Governance / Company Secretarial (1-2)
  ├── Internal Audit (2-3)
  ├── Corporate Communications / PR (2-3)
  ├── Facilities & Administration (2-5)
  ├── Procurement (1-2)
  └── CSR (1 + committee)
GOVERNANCE: Board (7-9). All committees active. Annual governance assessment.
```

### Stage 6: Public Company / Large Cap (500+ people)

```
YOU ARE: Listed company or operating at institutional scale.
ONGOING REQUIREMENTS:
□ Quarterly earnings reporting and analyst calls
□ Annual general meeting with shareholder voting
□ Continuous SEBI/SEC disclosure obligations
□ SOX/LODR compliance
□ Regular related party transaction monitoring
□ Insider trading window management
□ Annual board effectiveness evaluation
□ Succession planning for key roles
□ Enterprise risk management program
□ Business continuity management
□ Corporate sustainability reporting (ESG)
□ Stakeholder engagement program
□ M&A capability (evaluate and execute acquisitions)
□ Multi-geography compliance management
DEPARTMENTS: Full C-suite, each with 3-4 layers of management.
GOVERNANCE: Chair + board + committees + AGM + regulatory engagement + public accountability
```

## Scaling Inflection Points (When to Act)

```
| Trigger | Action Required |
|---------|----------------|
| First external money | Formalize cap table, SHA, board seat |
| 10 employees | POSH committee (India), employee handbook, HR policies |
| 20 employees | Dedicated HR person, PF registration, formal onboarding |
| 50 employees | Department heads, middle management, OKRs, internal comms |
| 100 employees | VP-level hires, internal audit, SOC 2, L&D program |
| 250 employees | Full C-suite, IPO preparation begins, governance formalization |
| 500+ employees | Institutional management, listed company compliance (if IPO) |
| ₹100Cr revenue | Statutory audit mandatory, GST e-invoicing, enhanced regulatory |
| ₹500Cr net worth | CSR mandatory (2% of avg net profit) (India) |
| International expansion | Transfer pricing, local entity, local compliance, multi-currency |
| IPO decision | 18-24 month preparation, board reconstitution, financial restatement |
```
