# 30-Day Launch Engine — Atlas UX Edition

## Purpose
Get Atlas UX from 0 to 10+ paying trade business customers in 30 days.
No budget. Content + outreach + Mercer.

---

## THE THREE DECISIONS (make before Day 1)

### Decision 1: Positioning

```
FOR trade business owners (salons, plumbers, HVAC, electricians)
WHO miss customer calls because they're doing the actual work
THIS IS a 24/7 AI receptionist
THAT answers calls, books appointments, and never misses a beat
UNLIKE voicemail (which customers hang up on) or human answering services ($200-600/mo)
WE cost $99/mo and Lucy never calls in sick
```

### Decision 2: First Vertical

Pick ONE trade vertical to dominate first. Recommended: **Salons/Barbershops**
- High call volume (5-15 calls/day)
- Appointment-based (Lucy can book directly)
- Moderate tech comfort
- Concentrated in cities (easy to find via Google Maps)
- Relatable scenario for video content

### Decision 3: Primary Channel

Pick ONE channel to go all-in on. Recommended: **Mercer cold calls + short-form video**
- Mercer is direct: calls trade businesses, books demos
- Video is scalable: one missed-call scenario video can get 100K views
- Both work with zero ad spend

---

## WEEK 1: FOUNDATION

```
DAY 1-2: LANDING PAGE FIX
□ Hero: "Never Miss Another Call" with trade-specific subheadline
□ Demo phone number: visitors call Lucy RIGHT NOW
□ Pricing: visible, simple, 3 tiers max
□ Trust: your photo, 1-sentence story, phone number
□ CTA: "Try Lucy Free for 14 Days — No Card Required"
□ Mobile-optimized (trade owners are on phones)

DAY 3-4: MERCER SETUP
□ Build call list: 200 salons in 5 metro areas (Google Maps scrape)
□ Write Mercer's script (see Agent 15 for messaging)
□ Test Mercer on 10 calls — refine script based on responses
□ Set up tracking: calls made, interested, demo booked, trial started

DAY 5-7: CONTENT PIPELINE
□ Write 5 missed-call scenarios (1 per trade vertical)
□ Produce in Canva: 30-60 second videos
□ Post first batch across TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, Facebook
□ Set up link-in-bio to landing page with UTM tracking
```

## WEEK 2: OUTREACH BLITZ

```
DAY 8-14: MERCER GOES LIVE
□ 50 calls/day minimum
□ Track daily: calls → interested → demos → trials
□ Adjust script based on what's working (which hook gets engagement?)
□ Follow up on all "call me back" responses within 24 hours

CONTENT CADENCE:
□ 1-2 short-form videos/day
□ Post in 5+ trade-specific Facebook groups (value first, not spam)
□ 1 livestream showing Lucy answering calls in real-time
□ Engage with EVERY comment and DM — these are warm leads

MILESTONE: First 3-5 trial signups by end of Week 2
If not: diagnose — Is Mercer calling? Is the landing page broken? Is the demo phone working?
```

## WEEK 3: CONVERSION FOCUS

```
DAY 15-21: TURN TRIALS INTO PAID
□ Personal check-in with every trial user on Day 3 and Day 7
□ Send them their call stats: "Lucy answered X calls this week"
□ Show ROI: "At your average job value, Lucy saved you $___"
□ Handle objections personally (Billy, not automated email)
□ First testimonial from first happy customer (text quote is fine)

MERCER CONTINUES: 50 calls/day
CONTENT CONTINUES: 1-2 videos/day

NEW: REFERRAL ASK
□ Ask every trial user: "Know any other [trade] owners who miss calls?"
□ Offer: "I'll give you and your referral $25 off next month"

MILESTONE: 3-5 paying customers by end of Week 3
```

## WEEK 4: SCALE WHAT WORKS

```
DAY 22-30: DOUBLE DOWN ON WINNERS
□ Which channel produced the most trials? Do more of that.
□ Which trade vertical converted best? Focus Mercer there.
□ Which video got the most views? Make 5 variations of it.
□ Which objection came up most? Create content addressing it.

CLOSE REMAINING TRIALS:
□ Trial ending text: "Your 14-day trial wraps tomorrow. Lucy answered
  [X] calls and booked [Y] appointments. Keep her for $99/mo?"
□ Personal call to anyone who hasn't converted
□ Last-day offer: "First month 50% off if you decide today" (optional)

MILESTONE: 10 paying customers by Day 30

POST-30-DAYS:
□ Collect testimonials from all paying customers
□ Write 1 case study with real numbers
□ Start SEO with blog posts
□ Evaluate: Should I expand to second vertical?
□ Evaluate: Is Mercer ROI positive? (demos booked per 100 calls)
```

## FAIL-SAFES

```
IF DAY 14 AND 0 TRIALS:
→ The product isn't resonating OR the channel isn't reaching people
→ Call 10 trade owners YOURSELF (not Mercer) and ask: "Would you pay $99/mo for a 24/7 receptionist?"
→ If they say no: your pricing or positioning is wrong
→ If they say yes but haven't signed up: your landing page or demo is broken

IF DAY 21 AND 0 PAID:
→ Trials aren't converting — the product experience has a problem
→ Call every trial user: "What would make you pay for this?"
→ Fix what they say, even if it's uncomfortable

IF DAY 30 AND < 5 PAID:
→ Either the vertical is wrong, the product isn't ready, or the pitch isn't landing
→ Don't throw money at ads — fix the foundation first
→ Consider: pivot vertical, adjust pricing, improve Lucy's call quality
```
