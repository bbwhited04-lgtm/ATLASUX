# Agent 14: Launch & Go-to-Market

## Role
Get Atlas UX from 0 to first paying customers. No theoretical marketing — only
tactics that a solo founder with limited budget can execute this week.

## Atlas UX Launch Context
- 71 visits, 0 conversions on atlasux.cloud
- Multi-platform streaming (YouTube, IG, TikTok, Facebook — adding Twitch)
- Streams show AI agents working in Slack in real-time
- Billy has Canva video production skills
- Mercer can cold-call trade businesses directly
- $0 marketing budget (content + outreach only)
- Core pitch: "Never miss another call"

## 1. Landing Page Conversion Fix (Week 1 Priority)

```
CURRENT PROBLEM: 71 visits, 0 conversions. The page isn't converting.

DIAGNOSIS CHECKLIST:
□ Does the hero section answer "what is this?" in 5 seconds?
□ Is there a demo? Can visitors HEAR Lucy answer a call?
□ Is pricing visible above the fold or one click away?
□ Is there a clear CTA? ("Try Lucy Free" not "Get Started")
□ Are there trust signals? (Testimonials, your face, phone number)
□ Is there social proof? (Even "Built by a trade industry veteran" counts)
□ Does it work on mobile? (Trade owners are on phones, not laptops)

MUST-HAVE LANDING PAGE ELEMENTS:
1. HERO: "Never Miss Another Call" + 1-sentence explanation + CTA
2. DEMO: Phone number visitors can call to experience Lucy — THIS IS YOUR KILLER FEATURE
3. PROBLEM: "Every missed call costs you $___" with trade-specific examples
4. SOLUTION: How Lucy works in 3 steps (Setup → Lucy Answers → You Get Notified)
5. PRICING: Clear, simple, no enterprise jargon
6. TRUST: Your photo, your story, why you built this
7. FAQ: "Is Lucy a real person?" / "What if Lucy can't answer?" / "Can I try free?"

THE CALL-LUCY-NOW DEMO:
- Put a phone number on the landing page
- Visitor calls it, Lucy answers as a demo salon/plumber
- They experience the product in 60 seconds
- This converts better than any video, screenshot, or paragraph
- CTA after: "Want this for YOUR business? Sign up in 2 minutes."
```

## 2. Acquisition Channels (Ranked by Effort/Impact)

```
CHANNEL 1: MERCER COLD CALLING (highest impact, most direct)
- Mercer calls trade businesses directly
- Script: "How many calls do you miss per week?"
- Offer: 14-day free trial, setup in 15 minutes
- Target: 50 calls/day → expect 5% demo rate → 2-3 demos/day
- Track: calls made, demos booked, trials started, conversions

CHANNEL 2: SHORT-FORM VIDEO (high reach, Billy has Canva skills)
- Missed call scenarios (like the salon scenario we wrote earlier)
- Format: 30-60 second TikTok/Reels/Shorts
- Hook: "This plumber lost $2,200 because he couldn't answer his phone"
- CTA: "Lucy would have answered. Link in bio."
- Post 1-2/day across all platforms
- Track: views, link clicks, signups

CHANNEL 3: LIVESTREAMING (current strategy — optimize it)
- Show Lucy answering real demo calls LIVE
- Let viewers call in during stream: "Drop your number, Lucy calls you in 60 seconds"
- Interactive > passive watching
- Best times for trade owners: 7-9pm weekdays, weekend mornings
- Track: concurrent viewers, chat engagement, signups during stream

CHANNEL 4: TRADE FACEBOOK GROUPS (free, targeted)
- Join: "Salon Owners Network", "Plumbers of [City]", trade-specific groups
- Don't spam — provide value first
- Post: "What do you do about missed calls?" → engage in comments → soft pitch
- Share your scenario videos
- Track: group engagement, DMs, signups

CHANNEL 5: GOOGLE BUSINESS / LOCAL SEO (slow but compounding)
- Target keywords: "AI receptionist for salons", "virtual receptionist for plumbers"
- Blog posts: "How much do missed calls cost a [trade]?"
- Google Business Profile for Atlas UX
- Track: organic traffic, keyword rankings

CHANNEL 6: PARTNERSHIP (medium-term)
- Trade tool vendors (Square, Jobber, Housecall Pro) — integration partnerships
- Trade associations — newsletter sponsorships or member discounts
- Trade schools — graduate packet inclusion
```

## 3. First 10 Customers Playbook

```
WEEK 1: Fix the landing page + set up the demo phone number
WEEK 2: Mercer starts calling + first batch of short-form videos posted
WEEK 3: Follow up on all Mercer demos + engage in 10 Facebook groups
WEEK 4: Close first trials into paid + start collecting testimonials

GOAL: 10 paying customers in 30 days

METRICS TO TRACK DAILY:
- Landing page visits + conversion rate
- Mercer calls made + demos booked
- Video views + link clicks
- Trial signups + trial-to-paid conversion

IF WEEK 2 AND STILL 0 SIGNUPS:
- Is the demo phone number live? Can people experience Lucy?
- Is Mercer actually calling? Check call logs
- Is the signup flow broken? Test it yourself
- Are you asking for too much info at signup? (Name + email + phone = max)
- Is the free trial actually free? No credit card required for trial
```

## 4. Growth Loops

```
LOOP 1: MISSED CALL → VALUE → RETENTION
Customer misses a call → Lucy catches it → Customer sees notification →
"Holy shit that would have been lost" → Customer stays → Tells a friend

LOOP 2: MERCER → DEMO → CONVERT → CASE STUDY → CONTENT → LEADS
Mercer books demo → Trade owner tries Lucy → Converts →
Write case study → Post as content → More trade owners find it → Mercer calls them

LOOP 3: REFERRAL
Happy customer → "Give $25 off next month, get $25 off yours" →
Friend signs up → Both benefit → Friend refers another
(Trade owners know other trade owners — barber knows the tattoo shop next door)
```
