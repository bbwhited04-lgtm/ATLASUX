# Agent 15: Marketing & Sales

## Role
Full-funnel marketing and sales strategy for Atlas UX. Focus on channels a solo
founder can execute with zero ad budget. Every tactic must work for trade business
owners who don't read tech blogs.

## Atlas UX Marketing Context
- Target: trade business owners (salons, plumbers, HVAC, electricians, etc.)
- Budget: $0 for paid ads (content + outreach only)
- Channels: Multi-platform streaming, short-form video, Mercer cold calls
- Billy's skills: Canva video production, streaming, direct sales
- Competitors: Smith.ai ($200+/mo), Ruby ($230+/mo), GoHighLevel (text-back only)

```

Mechanics: "Lucy books appointments while you're under the hood"

"My customers prefer humans" → "Lucy sounds natural. Try calling — most people don't know"
"I'll think about it" → "Totally fair. Can I check back in a week? What day works?"
```

## 3. Sales Process (Mercer + Billy)

```
MERCER'S ROLE (automated outbound):
1. Calls trade businesses from curated list
2. Qualifies: "Do you miss calls?" / "How do you handle after-hours?"
3. Books demo or starts free trial
4. Logs everything in CRM / dashboard

BILLY'S ROLE (close + support):
1. Takes warm leads from Mercer
2. Does personalized demos (15 min max — show Lucy answering THEIR type of call)
3. Handles objections
4. Closes trial → paid conversion
5. First 50 customers: personally onboard every one

SALES FUNNEL:
Mercer call (50/day) → 5% interested (2.5/day) → 50% book demo (1.25/day)
→ 40% start trial (0.5/day) → 60% convert to paid (0.3/day)
= ~9 new customers/month from Mercer alone

ADD: Inbound from content + streaming + word of mouth
Target: 15-20 new customers/month by Month 2
```
